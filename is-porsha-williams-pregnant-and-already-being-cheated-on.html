I'm Pregnant and My Baby Daddy Is Cheating!

This week, fans of The Real Housewives of Atlanta are still processing some odd news.

Porsha Williams is engaged to her co-star’s ex … after only one month of dating.

It’s super weird, and apparently he’s still legally married.

Is Porsha pregnant? And is she already getting cheated on?

To start at the beginning, Porsha Williams shared Instagram photos on Mother’s Day.

These photos included her ex, Dennis McKinley.

They also included a man named Simon Guobadia.

Simon is the ex of Friend of the Housewives Falynn Guobadia.

Porsha looked awfully cozy with Simon in the photos.

And she also seemed to be wearing a ring that had the look of an engagement ring.

Additionally, Porsha was wearing a flowing, carefree dress.

Some people were that sort of thing in every photo, but that’s never been Porsha.

All together … fans wanted to know what was up, and if that included a new baby.

“She’s gotta be pregnant. She looked it on her Mother’s Day post,” theorized a Reddit denizen.

“Pregnancy glow?” asked another when commenting on Porsha looking "amazing."

Scores of commenters observed that, to their eyes, she seemed to have a baby bump.

Questions were not confined only to the dread realm of Reddit, either.

Fans impertinently asked direct questions of Porsha, trying to see if she was pregnant.

Porsha has not, however, given a public comment on the pregnancy rumors.

What Porsha has addressed, however, is her new relationship with Simon.

“Our relationship began a month ago – yes we are crazy in love," she told fans.

"I know it’s fast but we are living life each day to its fullest," Porsha added.

"I choose happiness every morning and every night," Porsha affirmed.

She wrote that she is "tuning out all negative energy and only focused on positive wishes."

Porsha gushed: "He makes me so happy and to me, that is what matters most.

Porsha also clarified that she did not stab a friend in the back by dating her ex-husband.

“Falynn and I are not friends, and Simon’s divorce has been settled," Porsha wrote.

"Our relationship is a positive, loving step forward in everybody’s lives,” she proclaimed.

However, though Porsha and Simon both posted about their engagement, they were not the only ones to do so.

Falynn herself took to Instagram to share that she was still in the process of finalizing her divorce.

The marriage may be over, but on paper … it sounds like things weren’t as far along as Porsha believed.

Just hours after the engagement announcement, a woman came forward with apparent videos of herself in Simon’s car.

(Every report on this notes that it is a Ferrari, so we’ll also note what breed of car it is)

Jessica Harris is seen blowing kisses and sticking out her tongue in a slideshow shared on Instagram.

Jessica captioned one photo: "He moving faster than his car!"

She also appeared to be holding Simon’s jacket in one photo.

Another photo, tagged just five weeks ago, had the location tagged as "Simon’s car."

A series of alleged DMs between Jessica and a friend of hers seemed to confirm that she and Simon were "kinda" in a relationship.

According to DMs attributed to Jessica, the two of them met on March 30.

“He spent the night. We were planning travel and talked multiple times a day. Dinner, clubs and quality included,” one DM read.

Now, it’s possible that all of those good times were before — albeit, right before — Simon and Porsha began dating.

The timeline is a little fuzzy for the rest of us because we’re not Porsha and haven’t lived her life.

But Porsha already had a child with one cheater. Fans are anxious at the idea that she might do so again.
