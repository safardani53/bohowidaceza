Are Torrey Devitto and Yaya DaCosta leaving Chicago Med?

HEADING into its seventh season, the popular tv drama Chicago Med will be losing some cast members.

Originally airing in 2015, Chicago Med has seen many cast members come and go, and two original cast members are set to depart.

Are Torrey Devitto and Yaya DaCosta leaving Chicago Med?

Torrey Devitto and Yaya DaCosta, play Dr. Natalie Manning and ER nurse April Sexton respectfully.

Both have been with the show since it's inception back in 2015, but are not renewing their contracts ahead of the shows seventh season.

For Devitto, it is not the first time she will venture outside Chicago Med, as she has appeared in crossover episodes with Chicago Fire and Chicago P.D.

For DaCosta, she has lined up a new starring role on Fox’s Our Kind Of People.

The two will be the only original cast members set to depart, with the remaining set to return.

As season six of Chicago Med. finished, it marked the shows first year of their three year deal.

According to NBC's metrics, Chicago Med. was their most popular show this year.

With the show anchoring the network's Wednesday night lineup, it will be interesting to see if viewership is affected.

Devitto will be leaving the Chicago tv universe to join the indie feature Skelly.

Skelly is a film written and directed by Matthew Greene-DeLange and produced by Rob Goodrich.

Devitto joins a cast already featuring John Palladino, Brian Cox and Judah Abner Paul.

Skelly is about an 11-year-old whose grandfather dies plunging his dad into a state of grief that the boy can’t comprehend.

The boy then becomes obsessed with mortality and he and his friends put together the annual haunted house. 

Devitto plays the boy's mother and the movie's filming is slated to begin in late May in Massachusetts.

Most reasd in the chicago universe

With two of the original cast members having left, fans are wondering if this will mark a new era in the Chicago universe.

With new cast members can often come new directions, and character drama is what fans live for.

Chicago Med. can be seen on NBC at 8pm EST on Wednesday nights, along with the rest of the Chicago lineup.
