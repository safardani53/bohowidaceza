Amber Rose Poses in Barely There G-String Swimsuit: NSFW Pictures!

Amber Rose left little to the imagination on Saturday, Jan. 17, when she posed on a balcony in a black G-string swimsuit. The model, 31, shared four racy images on Instagram, which showed off her curvy body after giving birth to son Sebastian in February 2013. 

She captioned the images, "Fleekin," "MILFIN," "Slayin," and "Stifler's Mom," referencing the Mrs. Robinson-esque character in the American Pie franchise. 

Rose filed for divorce from her rapper ex Wiz Khalifa last September, but has remained positive throughout. 

In October she talked to Us Weekly about the split, saying, "I'm okay. I'm doing pretty good. I am going through a divorce and technically I'm single I guess you could say, but I'm just not ready for that. I'm focused on my son, and I'm focused on work, and I really don't have time for a man right now." 

Later that same month, Rose responded to a birthday tweet from Khalifa, writing, "@wizkhalifa Thank u Sweetheart #StillLoveYouNoMatterWhat."
