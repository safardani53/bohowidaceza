HOLIDAY SPIRIT

The Kardashians have been getting in the holiday spirit and the family always goes all-out.

From Kylie's huge Christmas tree to Kourtney's humble decorations, the family members pull out all the stops at their Los Angeles mansions.

This year, however, the competition has been complicated by a new arrival, which has also upended a long-standing family legacy.

Earlier this month, Khloe announced that there will not be a family Christmas card this year.

She made a post on her Snapchat Story explaining why the tradition has ended.

Khloe wrote: "As our family grew it got a little trickier (anyone with kids knows how impossible it is to get multiple kids to look at the camera lol).

"And so we decided to leave that tradition in the past for now, but I'm all about taking as many photos as we can."

In the post, the Good American founder used a version of the 2021 Christmas card featuring Kim and their mother Kris.

The photo also showed four of the Kardashian kids, and only one of them was looking at the camera.

However, just because the family cards may be canceled, the family has still gone all out on other aspects of the holiday.

NEW TRADITIONS

Khloe showed off the elaborate Christmas decorations inside her mansion as she continues to compete with her sisters.

The Hulu star took to her Instagram Stories to show off the over-the-top decor inside the dining room of her $17 million Hidden Hills mansion.

Khloe panned her massive dining table that featured a full-length piece of garland going down the center.

The gorgeous centerpiece was accompanied by various, dark-colored candles that were lit throughout.

The Good American founder then gave a peak at another table that was pushed up against the wall, which was decorated with a similar garland.

The long wreath of leaves was intertwined with twinkling white holiday lights and had a few decorative, shimmering Christmas trees placed around.
