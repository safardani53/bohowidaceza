Hilary Duff Through The Years: Photos Hollywood Life

Can you believe Hilary Duff is 36? See photos of the ‘Lizzie McGuire’ star through the years here.

Hilary Duff, born September 28, 1987, is an American singer and actress. Hilary rose to fame on the Disney Channel series ‘Lizzie McGuire,’ which ran from 2001-2004. In that time, she also played the titular role  in the feature-length film, which came out in 2003! 

Throughout her career, Hilary has found success in both singing and acting. After she became a total teen sensation, Hilary started searching for more mature roles. Between 2015-2019, she appeared on the TV Land series ‘Younger,’ and in 2015 released her fourth studio album ‘Breathe In. Breath Out.’ Now, Hilary is a married mother of two, who continues to accomplish more and more every day! To see more photos of Hilary through the years, check out the rest of the photos in this gallery by scrolling through the images below!
