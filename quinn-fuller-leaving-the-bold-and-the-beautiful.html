Why did Quinn leave The Bold and the Beautiful?

SIMILAR to the other Bold and the Beautiful characters, Quinn Fuller's storyline was packed with drama.

After nine seasons on the hit soap opera, Quinn's actress Rena Sofer departed the long-running series.

Why did Quinn leave The Bold and the Beautiful?

In July 2013, General Hospital actress Rena Sofer made her debut as Quinn Fuller on The Bold and The Beautiful.

On August 17, 2022, Rena revealed that her time with B&B had ended.

Over Instagram and Twitter, she wrote: "Friday, August 5 was not just my daughter's birthday but the end of my 9year run as Quinn on @boldandbeautifulcbs.

"I know it’s heartbreaking for some and it is for me as well, but it’s time for me to move on with both my acting career and my pottery career.

"Thank you all for your support over the last 9 years and in the future! And check out Renasoferceramics.com for the other side of my life!!"

During an interview with Soap Opera Digest, Rena explained why she decided to leave the long-running series.

While discussing her departure, she said: "Now is my time to see what’s out there for me.

"I had already decided this was going to be my last contract, but when they finished the story of Quinn and Carter coming together, it seemed like the right time."

On August 29, 2022, viewers said goodbye to Quinn as her exit unfolded during her last episode which was broadcasted that Monday.

Who is Quinn's actress Rena Sofer?

In season 26 of B&B, Quinn was introduced to the residents of Los Angeles by her son Wyatt Spencer.

Regarding her character's storyline, Emmy Award-winning actress Rena shared with the above-mentioned outlet: "I think her signature storyline was trying to ruin Liam’s connection with Steffy and Hope in order to save them for her son.

"That would be a signature of why everyone hated Quinn from the get-go.

"Yes, she does some stuff and she makes mistakes, but I like that."

The Melrose Place alum added: "Don’t we all make mistakes? There’s nothing I would change about her.

"She has really become a full-fledged human being, and I’m very proud to play her as I get to do.

"First of all, I can’t believe it’s been nine years. But I think the best thing I’ve loved about being a part of this show is being a part of the legacy of Bold and Beautiful."

How can I watch B&B?

From Monday to Friday, CBS airs new episodes of B&B daily.

The show broadcasts on weekdays at 1.30pm EST and is available on streaming services afterward.
