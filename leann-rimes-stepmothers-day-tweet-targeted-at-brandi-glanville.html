Stepmother's Day Tweet Targeted at Brandi Glanville?

We thought the trailer for the LeAnn Rimes, Eddie Cibrian reality show was the most ridiculous thing on the Internet, but it turns out that title may belong to LeAnn’s Twitter page. 

LeAnn has apparently discovered something called Stepmother’s Day and since it presents an opportunity for her to take subtle shots at her longtime rival Brandi Glanville, she’s all about it!

"Mother’s Day vs. Stepmother’s Day: Who Owns It?" LeAnn tweeted yesterday along with a link to an article about the corny new holiday.

We can understand why a stepmom of two such as LeAnn would appreciate a holiday of her own (Though can’t you just buy your mom and stepmom the same crappy card on Mother’s Day and be done with it?), but the way she’s pitted the two days against one another feels like a shot at Brandi.

 

Naturally, some followers pointed out that LeAnn’s tweet (and the telling link to Huffington Post Divorce) may be construed as an inappropriate diss directed at Brandi, LeAnn responded as she usually does: she got pissed!

"Do some people only read headlines these days?" LeAnn tweeted. "There is a Stepmother’s day, let’s celebrate it…THAT’S ALL!"

Even if the tweet really was completely innocent and she only intended to point out that the holiday exists, surely someone like LeAnn who’s lived her entire life in the spotlight could’ve guessed that some might take her comment as a continuation of her very public feud with Brandi.

After all, last we heard from these two, LeAnn was loving Brandi’s meltdown, so we’re guessing they’re not happily co-parenting a few weeks later.

In case you’re wondering Stepmother’s Day is apparently on May 19 this year. We’re sure LeAnn is super mad that it comes after Mother’s Day.
