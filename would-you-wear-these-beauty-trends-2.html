Would You Wear These Beauty Trends?

HEADBAND OVER BANGS

The headband trend is here to stay, and Carrie Underwood and Miley Cyrus show off its latest incarnation – slipping a band on top of their long bangs.

SLEEK BOBS

Spring is the perfect time to chop inches off dull hair – just look what it’s done for Liv Tyler and Gwyneth Paltrow!

CLEOPATRA LINER

No one has ever accused these style icons of blending into the crowd! Christina Aguilera and Madonna show off their fierce signature style with exaggeratedly feline eyeliner.

SWEPT OVER COIFF

Beyoncé and Paris Hilton get swept up in an ’80s retro moment, adding volume to their roots and then brushing their locks up and over the crown.

CAT-EYE EYELINER

Nicole Richie and Keira Knightley wing out with their dramatic extended black liner on their upper lashlines.

CENTER SWEEP-BACK

No need to worry about bangs blocking your vision with this style! Ashley Tisdale and Jada Pinkett Smith keep the focus on their faces by pulling hair over the crown and letting the sides cascade down.

VEILS

Jennifer Lopez and Dita VonTeese go undercover in elegant tulle veils that give a nod to silver screen sirens of the ’40s.

BOUFFANT BOB

Maria Menounos and Eva Longoria Parker go for retro glamour by amping up the volume of their bobs.

HALF UP, HALF DOWN DO

Lauren Conrad and Rachel Bilson give their long locks an instant makeover by twisting back their bangs on either side of their center part and pinning them in place.

EXTRA-LONG EXTENSIONS

Rumer Willis, Selma Blair and Lauren Conrad add instant inches to their coifs with super-straight hair extensions.

HIGH PONYTAILS

Giving a fresh update to a standard style, Lucy Liu and Lauren Conrad take the ponytail to new heights – keeping it at the crown of their heads.

GREEN EYESHADOW

No boring neutrals for these stars – Kristen Bell, Kate Hudson and Thandie Newton play up their eyes with bold shadow in verdant shades.

SIDE-SWEPT WAVES

Flipping their long brown locks over one shoulder, Jessica Alba and Katharine McPhee offer a new take on letting their hair down.

BOUFFANT PONYTAILS

Eva Mendes, Kristen Bell and Ashlee Simpson pump up their ponytails’ volume with teased crowns.

JEWELED HEADBANDS

Emmy Rossum and Natalie Portman add a dash of glamour to their updos with thin sparkling bands.

BRAIDED UPDO

Anne Hathaway, Sarah Michelle Gellar and Jessica Alba add a goddess touch to their updos with tight braids wrapping around the sides.

BLUE UNDER-EYES

Kate Hudson and Katie Holmes play up their eyes with bright slashes of blue just under their bottom lashes.

BEADED HEADBANDS

Skip the necklace and go for a baubled band instead – just like Mary-Kate Olsen and Vanessa Hudgens.

PIN CURLS

Diane Kruger and Natalie Portman add old-fashioned flair to their updos with pinned-up spit curls.

PURPLE EYESHADOW

Rihanna, Katie Holmes and Ellen Page are getting a jump on spring’s pretty pastels with vibrant violet shadow.

STUDDED HEADBANDS

The preppy favorite gets an edgy update with bold gold studs, as spotted on Lauren Conrad and Maria Menounos.

HOT PINK LIPS

How do these hit singers keep all eyes on their lips? Rihanna, Alicia Keys and Lily Allen paint their pouts fuchsia.

TWO-TONE HAIR

Gina Gerson and Lake Bell give new meaning to roots with their fade-to-blonde color.

LASH-SWEEPING BANGS

Kim Kardashian and Kerry Washington show off identical dos, with extra-long bangs and soft, full curls.

BEEHIVE UPDO

Eva Mendes and Beyoncé are bringing back the ’50s, glamming up with the decade’s most iconic updo.

ROMANTIC SIDE BUN

Twisting their wavy hair into loose side buns, Miley Cyrus and Molly Sims bring Pre-Raphaelite elegance to the red carpet.

GLOSSY NUDE LIPS

Halle Berry and Becki Newton aren’t scared to go nude, with high-shine lips in neutral shades.

PEEK-A-BOO BANGS

The gorgeous Jessicas might have nothing to hide, but Simpson and Alba’s one-eyed hairstyles give a new view to bangs.

SIDE BRAID

Emmy Rossum and Mary-Kate Olsen let down their locks in messy single braids tossed to the front.
