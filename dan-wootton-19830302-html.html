Daniel John William Wootton is a New Zealand journalist and radio host. He is the editor-in-chief of The Sun newspaper. In 2007, he joined News of the World. In 2013, he joined The Sun on Sunday and became editor-in-chief of the Bizarre column the following year. In February 2016, he became associate editor of The Sun and in March 2018 was promoted to editor. In 2021, Wootton will be leaving News UK to join MailOnline as a columnist and host a show on GB News. Wootton has appeared as a business presenter on ITV Lorraine and Daybreak Breakfast. From 2015 to 2018, he was also a regular contributor and attending seminars on Big Brother's Bit on the Side.
