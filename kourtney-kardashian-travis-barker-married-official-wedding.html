Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker 'MARRY' in Santa Barbara one month after unofficial Las Vegas

KOURTNEY Kardashian and Travis Barker have officially married.

Reports of their Santa Barbara I do's came one month after their unofficial Las Vegas ceremony.

Kourtney, 43, and Travis, 46, are now husband and wife, according to TMZ and others.

On Sunday, the pair were spotted in Santa Barbara, California, exchanging vows at the downtown courthouse steps there.

They wore traditional bride and groom attire as they were seen outside the building.

Travis wore an all-black tuxedo, and Kourtney a knee-length white dress, with long white sleeves that reached her hands - and a veil.

They continued to keep things classic by riding off in a black Chevrolet convertible that sweetly read: "JUST MARRIED."

According to Page Six, along with a few bodyguards as witnesses, there were some family members in tow.

Travis' father, Randy, was in the mix, as was Kourtney's grandmother Mary Jo Campbell.

Sources close to the couple told TMZ: "Yes, they got the legal paperwork in order Sunday -- so they are, in fact, married now.

"They're going to be having an actual wedding ceremony in Italy in the near future."

An insider also shared to PEOPLE: "They had to legally get married first ahead of their big Italian wedding, which is happening very soon.

"All the details are set and the whole family, including all the kids, are very excited."

The happily now-married couple appeared to chat with a few people on the street before cruising away.

But not before a Twitter user caught them mid-moment and gushed: "I inadvertently walked right by Kourtney and Travis' wedding photo shoot today!"

Kourtney's mom Kris Jenner, 66, said last week she was "sworn to secrecy" on all wedding details, per PEOPLE - so it would seem she was aware of the big decision.

She told the mag: "If I say one thing about a wedding, I am gonna be in so much trouble!"

No family members nor reps have confirmed the news as of yet.

'PRACTICE MAKES PERFECT'

The momentous occasion comes after the lovebirds got married "just for fun" in April, 2022.

Kourtney and Travis had unofficially married at a Las Vegas chapel with a handful of friends at 2 am - hours after the Blink-182 rocker performed at the 2022 Grammys.

The mom of three later admitted they are not yet legally married as they couldn't get a marriage license at that time in the morning.

A source close to the stars told The Sun that indeed, the event was held "just for fun" and not official.

In true Vegas fashion, Kourtney and Travis had an Elvis Presley impersonator officiate the earlier non-binding wedding.

Marty Frierson, owner of One Love Wedding Chapel, earlier told People at thetime: "I didn't know who it was until they pulled up. I advertise as 24-hours but they wanted to make sure I was still there.

"They paid and they requested Elvis Presley, that was mandatory. I called back 5 minutes later and was like, I got an Elvis, and there they were."

He added: "They came, got married, tossed the bouquet in the driveway, and danced to Elvis. They showed a lot love and had a lot of fun."

The ceremony was said to have lasted about 30 minutes and no video was allowed to be taken - but photos certainly were.

Alongside a series of PDA-packed pics from their "practice" wedding, Kourtney wrote to Instagram days after: "Found these in my camera roll.

"Once upon a time in a land far, far away (Las Vegas) at 2am, after an epic night and a little tequila, a queen and her handsome king ventured out to the only open chapel with an Elvis and got married (with no license).

"Practice makes perfect."

BEAUTIFUL NEW CHAPTER

In late April, Kourtney and Travis appeared to have a wedding shower, per fans, based on flowery photos to Instagram.

Travis shares two children with his ex-wife Shanna Moakler - Landon, 18, and Alabama, 16.

Kourtney shares three kids, Mason, 12, Penelope, 9, and Reign, 7, with ex of 10 years Scott Disick, 38.

Although the pair does not currently have any biological children together, their blended family is a large one - and they have shown their IVF pregnancy efforts on Hulu's The Kardashians.

Back in October 2021, the drummer proposed to the famous reality star at a beachside hotel in Montecito, as seen on the now-married Kourtney's Hulu show.

Present for the special moment were sisters Kim, Khloe, Kylie Jenner, and Kendall Jenner as Travis proposed on the beach, surrounded by dozens of flowers and candles.
