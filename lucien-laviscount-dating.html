Who Is 'Emily in Paris' Hunk Lucien Laviscount Dating?

'Emily in Paris' star Lucien Laviscount is quite a catch. Is he currently in a relationship? Here's everything we know about his love life.

Now that Emily in Paris is back on Netflix for its third season, fans are preparing to dive back into the frothy, silly fun that the show can often provide. Last season, Emily in Paris introduced Lucien Laviscount, who plays Alfie, a British banker who becomes part of Emily's romantic entanglements.

And while we already know that Emily and Alfie hit it off on the show (wink wink), what is Lucien's love life like off camera? Here's everything we know.

Is Lucien Laviscount single?

Lucien appears to currently be single. The 30-year-old British actor has seemingly never married, and based on his scattered dating history, it looks like he hasn't really ever had a long-term partner either.

Who has Lucien Laviscount dated?

Lucien is quite a catch if you ask us! And although he doesn't share too much about his personal life on social media, we were able to dig up some info on his former lovers.

Lucien reportedly dated Big Brother winner Sophie Reade in 2010 and was linked a year later to his Waterloo Road co-star Chelsea Healey. Lucien was also spotted on a dinner date with Little Mix member Leigh-Anne Pinnock in 2012. The following year, he was also rumored to be dating actress Brooke Vincent.

Article continues below advertisement

Furthermore, Lucien was also romantically linked to his Scream Queens co-star Keke Palmer in 2015. And the Daily Mail spotted Lucien holding hands with Kelly Osborne in 2019. More recently, Lucien has been linked to a slew of other women. And in November 2021, Lucien was linked to Jesy Nelson, another (former) member of Little Mix. The two were spotted snuggling up against one another in a rickshaw in London.

A source later told The Sun that Jesy was in fact single and that the two weren't kissing in the photos, despite what it may have looked like. “They’re not boyfriend and girlfriend — they just ran into each other on a night out,” the source declared.

Even more recently, Lucien has been linked to three other potential romantic partners. He attended the British Fashion Awards and was seen linking arms with both Wallis Day of Batwoman and singer-songwriter Arlissa at the event.

At the same event, Lucien was also seen holding hands with former Game of Thrones star Hannah John-Kamen. It seems that Lucien's real-life romantic entanglements may be even more complicated than those of the character he plays on Emily in Paris.
