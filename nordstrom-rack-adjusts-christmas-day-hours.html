Nordstrom Rack adjusts Christmas hours at all 348 stores to give staff time off but won't completely

NORDSTROM Rack has announced their Christmas hours as the holiday approaches.

In order to give their employees time to spend with family and friends over the holidays, Nordstrom Rack stores will be closed for Christmas.

CHRISTMAS CLOSURE

Across the US, hundreds of Nordstrom Rack stores will be closed for Christmas.

The U.S. Sun reached out to several locations to confirm their store hours for other important days over holidays.

A few stores said that they will be open regular hours on Christmas Eve.

Others mentioned opening at their regular time on Christmas Eve but closing a few hours early.

Read More on Nordstrom Rack

Regarding New Year's Eve, some stores will be open regular hours while others may close early.

Be sure to check with your local Nordstrom Rack as holiday hours will vary by location.

OTHER RETAIL GIANTS

Other major retailers have come out with their hours for the holidays.

Kohl's will also close its 1,100 stores on Christmas Day.

However, their locations will likely be open on Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve.

Last year most Kohl's stores were open from 8 am to 6 pm on these two holidays.

Walmart is another retail giant that will be closed for Christmas Day so their employees can have time off.

They will be open on Christmas Eve, but at reduced hours.

Additionally, Target stores across the US will be closed on Christmas Day.

They will, however, be open on Christmas Eve.
