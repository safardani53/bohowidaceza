Stars Wearing Plaid See Celebrity Fashion Hollywood Life

With fall officially here, some of our favorite celebrities can’t get enough of the hottest trend of the season – plaid. From blazers to pants and mini skirts, plaid is such a versatile print and there’s a variety of ways to style the pattern, which the stars have proven. Kendall Jenner, 25, tried out the trend when she grabbed coffee in LA on March 9. The supermodel rocked a pair of black Kendall + Kylie Move on Sweatpants with a white tank top and a bright red and blue Cherry Quilted Flannel Shirt Jacket on top, which was super baggy and oversized. She accessorized her casual outfit with a pair of crisp white Nike Air Force 1 Sneakers, an Alexander Wang Chain Mesh Rhinestone Pouch, and her fave pair of Kim Kardashian West x Carolina Lemke Dusk Sunglasses.

Gigi Hadid, 25, was out in NYC on March 3 when she rocked a pair of high-waisted tan trousers with white flannel panels on the front. She styled the pants with a purple and brown plaid Vivienne Westwood Fall 2020 long peacoat with a cropped vest version underneath. She accessorized her patterned outfit with a pair of black Dr. Martens 1460 Boots, Amavii Benjamin Sunglasses, a Ferragamo Ostrich Bag, and a Victorinox Connex Carry-On Suitcase.

One of our other favorite models, Hailey Baldwin, 24, was out in West Hollywood on January 29 when she rocked a tight tan Set Active Ribbed V Bra with baggy high-waisted gray drawstring sweatpants and a super oversized orange flannel S.K Manor Hill Plaid Shirt on top, which she chose to leave open. She accessorized with a pair of orange & yellow Nike x Offwhite Dunk Low University Sneakers, Saint Laurent New Wave Sl299 Sunglasses, chunky gold Jennifer Fisher Mini Maeve Hoop Earrings, and a bright red Louis Vuitton Supreme Keepall Bandouliere Bag.

Another one of our favorite celebs to try out the look was Jennifer Lopez, 51, who dressed up the look. JLo arrived at Good Morning America on Sept. 10, when she wore a skintight black, tan and brown Monse Asymmetric Plaid Crepe Midi Dress, which featured a plunging slit on her thigh, revealing her toned legs. On top of her dress, she wore a floor-length camel duster coat and she accessorized with a Le Vian Honey Gold Ring, nude suede Jimmy Choo Anouk Pumps, a Max Mara Grace Bag, and Nile Custom Sunglasses.

Hailey also tried out the trend when she was out in LA on Oct. 12, wearing a super oversized gray plaid Balenciaga Suspender Shoulder Jacket which she styled with gray sweatpants and an Aritzia The Oversized Crew Light sweatshirt. She dressed up her casual look with a pair of pointed gray heeled Fenty Jersey Corset Boots, and Oliver Peoples Ov5417Su Jaye Sunglasses.

Another celeb who pulled off the look perfectly was Rita Ora, 30, when she was out in London on Sept. 19. Rita looked super chic in a flowy black Marc Jacobs Plaid Strap Dress with a Marc Jacobs Mini-Striped Long-Sleeve crop top over the dress. She topped her look off with a cropped ivory Marc Jacobs Satin Trim Blazer, a Marc Jacobs Rainbow Striped Knit Beanie, bright yellow Adidas x Oyster Holdings Samba Og Sneakers, and Celine Cl41468 Transparent Blue Cat-Eye Sunglasses.

There have been so many other celebs who have looked fabulous in plaid which you can see when you click through the gallery above!
