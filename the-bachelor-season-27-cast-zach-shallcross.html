The Bachelor 2023: Who is in the cast of season 27?

ZACH Shallcross is preparing to find love on this season of The Bachelor.

With season 27 set to debut on January 23, 2023, fans want to know more about the ladies competing for his heart.

Who is in the cast of season 27?

On January 5, 2023, ABC announced the lineup for this season of The Bachelor.

"There's no words, I'm just taking this in right now," Shallcross said of the casting via PEOPLE.

"I'm obviously really nervous right now but this is once in a lifetime."

The contestants include:

Becca is a 25-year-old Nursing Student from Burbank, California, who loves the outdoors and is a sucker for a good romance novel.

"Becca is incredible. She is a nursing student who has always put others before herself and dedicates her life to helping people. She comes from a close-knit Mexican American family and was raised by her mom, who she says is the person she loves most in this world," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Brianna is a boss woman looking to add some love to her life! She is smart, entrepreneurial and creative, which lead to her owning a successful beauty company at just 24 years old," her Bachelor bio reads.

Brianna has high expectations, but she's truly hopeful that Zach could be the man for her!"

Brooklyn

"Brooklyn is a rodeo country girl ready to saddle up for the love story of a lifetime! At just 13 years old, Brooklyn discovered her passion for horse barrel racing and went on to study animal science," her Bachelor bio reads.

Will Zach be the perfect cowboy for this fun-loving cowgirl? Only time will tell."

Gabi is a 25-year-old Account Executive from Pittsford, Vermont, who loves to tailgate and considers Kris Jenner her favorite Kardashian.

Genevie

Genevie is a 26-year-old Neonatal Nurse from Baltimore City, Maryland, who loves the Baltimore Ravens and can "kind of" drive a stick shift.

"Genevie is definitely the full package, with a smile that lights up every room she walks into! Not only is she a registered nurse, but she's hoping to be CEO of a hospital one day. Boss moves," her Bachelor bio reads.

The Maryland native is looking for a loyal and adventurous man and only dates to find the real deal. When Genevie loves, she loves hard and is looking for the right partner to settle down with."

Greer

Greer is a 24-year-old Medical Sales Rep from Houston, Texas, who loves flea markets and never gets embarrassed.

"Greer is a bold, hilarious woman who isn't afraid to speak her mind. She was born and raised in Houston, Texas, but took a risk and moved to New York City, where she loves living life to the fullest," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Greer is loyal and confident and is looking for a partner who can keep up with her quick wit and big personality. She is a hopeless romantic and is a sucker for forehead kisses. Zach, take notes! Will Greer and Zach have the love story of a lifetime?"

Holland

Holland is a 24-year-old Insurance Marketer from Boca Raton, Florida, who loves yachting and The Bachelor.

"Holland is a spunky and confident woman who needs a man who can keep up with her energy! The top things Holland is looking for in a relationship are unwavering commitment, loyalty and a genuine personality. Oh, and he also MUST love wine and sushi," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Pilates and spending time at the beach in her home state of Florida are also important parts of Holland's life, and it would be a huge plus if she could meet someone to enjoy her favorite things with her. Holland knows what she wants, and good luck to anyone who tries to stop her!"

Jessica "Jess"

Jess is a 23-year-old E-Commerce Coordinator from Winter Springs, Florida, who loves playing Bananagrams and always leaves the house with sunscreen.

"Jess is a sweet soul from Orlando, Florida, who says Zach is her dream man! While Jess is one of the younger women, she is mature, knows what she wants in a partner and doesn't want to settle for anything less than her soul mate," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Jess describes herself as a hopeless romantic and is ready for her fairytale romance worthy of a Taylor Swift love song. Jess comes from a big Puerto Rican family and hopes Zach is ready for her to spice up his life. Jess hopes Zach is as excited about her as she is about him!"

Kaitlyn "Kaity"

Kaity is a 27-year-old ER Nurse from Austin, Texas, who loves roller coasters and isn't afraid to slide into the DMs.

"Kaity is 100% wife material! The Canadian nurse is not only passionate about her career but she's got a great sense of humor and is extremely down to earth. Kaity is looking for her forever Prince Charming and says, 'I truly hope to find love. I have so much love to give to the right man,'" her Bachelor bio reads.

"Hopefully, Zach is the right man for her! Kaity can't wait to start a family and said that if a man didn't want kids, that would be a dealbreaker for her. Will Zach be the one to show this beauty that true love is out there? Only time will tell."

Katherine

Katherine is a 26-year-old RN from Tampa, Florida, who often gets compared to Julia Roberts and loves breakfast pastries.

"Katherine is a vibrant go-getter with a witty sense of humor and a glass-half-full attitude. She loves spending time with her nieces and nephews and can't wait to have a family of her own. Katherine's career as a registered nurse is extremely important to her, but her No. 1 priority is to find her perfect match," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Katherine's dream man is loyal, trustworthy, a great communicator and, most importantly, her best friend. She is looking for a real man who will enhance her life, and she's ready to put it all on the line to pursue love."

Kimberly

Kimberly is a 30-year-old Hospitality Manager from Los Angeles, California, who can't wait to be a mom and loves old-school hip-hop music.

"Kimberly is not here to mess around! She was in a serious relationship that lasted six years, but she was ready for her forever, and her ex was not. Kimberly has been single for the past few years and doesn't want just to date around. She is waiting for her perfect man," her Bachelor bio reads.

"The Puerto Rican beauty's ideal date night would include watching the sunset at the beach with a picnic dinner. Zach, save that idea for later! Kimberly is ready to find her husband and start a family of her own. She is excited about the chance to fall in love again and can't wait for her 'Bachelor' journey to begin!"

Kylee

Kylee is a 25-year-old Postpartum Nurse from Charlotte, North Carolina, who loves buttery popcorn with M&Ms and has a crush on San Francisco 49ers quarterback Jimmy Garoppolo.

"Kylee may best be known as an NBA dancer, but this girl has way more going on than just her killer dance moves and beautiful smile! Outside of her love of dancing, Kylee is a registered nurse who works in a postpartum unit. She is passionate about helping new mothers and can't wait to have a baby of her own ASAP," her Bachelor bio reads.

"She may only be 25, but Kylee is ready for the soccer mom life, so hopefully, Zach is on the same page! Kylee admits that she falls in love hard and fast, so time will tell if this hopeless romantic will be able to find her soul mate in Zach."

Chandralekha "Lekha"

Lekha is a 29-year-old Financial Advisor from Miami, Florida, who loves massages and abs.

"Lekha is a woman of the world! She was born in Chennai, India, and moved to the United States at 7 years old. Since then, she's lived in California, North Carolina, New York and Florida. It may take a little while for Lekha to come out of her shell, but her exuberant personality and top-notch belly dancing skills are worth the wait," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Lekha is ready to find 'the one,' and while her parent's marriage was arranged, they are supportive of her journey to find love and happiness in her own way! Her close-knit family has her excited to start a family of her own. She can't wait to meet Zach and says, 'I want nothing more than to be a great mom and find my person to share a life with.'"

Madison

Madison is a 26-year-old Business Owner from Fargo, North Dakota, who was a Presidential Scholar in college and does not like sauce.

"Madison is ready to receive the love she gives to so many others! While growing up, Madison took on the role of caretaker for her two younger sisters. So, when it comes to her love life, her little sisters are part of a package deal, and Madison wouldn't have it any other way," her Bachelor bio reads.

"She hopes to meet an honest, empathetic, loyal man with a caring family. Madison has worked extremely hard to get to such a great place in life and says she is more than ready to find someone to share it with. She is serious about finding love on this journey, so Madison, let the journey begin."

Mercedes

Mercedes is a 24-year-old Non-Profit Owner from Bloomfield, Iowa, who often drives around blasting country music and is not a fan of the kitchen.

"Mercedes is a fun-loving, bubbly woman ready to spread her wings! She has four jobs and works extremely hard to support herself, but she is ready to prioritize finding her perfect match. Mercedes is very close with her mother and will definitely be looking for her approval the next time she brings home a man! Mercedes has been in two serious relationships, but both ended because she knew those men weren't her person," her Bachelor bio reads.

"While Mercedes loves a night out on the town in Nashville, she also enjoys staying in and cuddling with her dog. She says she's looking for a man who will make her laugh and is someone she can bring to family dinner on Sundays. Mercedes says her love language is giving and receiving gifts, so hopefully, Zach is ready to be showered with tokens of her love!"

Olivia L

Olivia L is a 24-year-old Patient Care Technician from Rochester, New York, who does not like litterbugs and was inspired by Grey's Anatomy.

"For Olivia L, basketball has always come easy. The one game she's yet to win is Oliva vs True Love, and this baller is here to find her teammate for LIFE! While basketball was her first love, she's built a life outside of her sport, working full-time in healthcare, and has her dreams set on helping others as a physician assistant," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Olivia has traveled to almost every state in the US but still hasn't met her perfect match. She is looking forward to meeting Zach and is hoping to find a relationship that will stand the test of time."

Olivia M

Olivia M is a 25-year-old Stylist from Cincinnati, Ohio, who loves reading and carbs.

"Born and raised in a small town with a loving family, Olivia M. is looking for the love of her life! She may be a city-slicker now, but Olivia will always be a small-town girl at heart. She studied fashion and has a passion for all things style and design," her Bachelor bio reads.

"When she's not working hard at her career, Olivia loves going to hot yoga, trying new restaurants and cuddling up on the couch to binge-watch TV. Olivia is hoping to meet a loyal, loving and driven man. Will Zach be her prince charming?"

Sonia

Sonia is a 29-year-old Project Manager from Long Island, New York, who is obsessed with Elvis and has been bungee jumping multiple times.

"Sonia is the life of the party! This outgoing Long Island girl is ready to settle down and put her dating days behind her. After multiple serious relationships, Sonia knows she's ready for marriage and hopes Zach is her future husband," her Bachelor bio reads.

"She says she's looking for a head-over-heels kind of love and truly believes her soul mate is out there. Sonia is extremely close with her family and hopes her future partner is as family-oriented as she is. And while she's a city girl, for now, Sonia is hoping to move to the suburbs one day with her man and raise three golden retrievers. Hopefully, Zach is ready to be a dog dad!"

Vanessa

Vanessa is a 23-year-old Restaurant Marketer from Baton Rouge, Louisiana, who loves cherries and played a lot of freeze tag as a child.

"Vanessa is ready to say goodbye to the single life! She is so excited to meet Zach and hopes he's the loyal, charismatic man she's been looking for. Vanessa works in marketing and strives to be as successful as marketing genius Kris Jenner one day," her Bachelor bio reads.

"We love a girl that dreams big! And while she's passionate about her work, Vanessa's priority right now is finding her life partner. She considers herself a top-notch foodie, so hopefully, Zach is hungry for good food and a good time! Will this be the end of Vanessa's search for love? Fingers crossed!"

Victoria J

Victoria J is a Makeup Artist from Fort Worth, Texas, who has a degree in dance performance and doesn't understand why everyone is obsessed with Pete Davidson.

"This Texas native is the full package! Ambitious and resilient, Victoria J. is ready to live life to the fullest! She is determined to take chances to finally find the true love she's been searching for. Her parents have been married for over 40 years, and Victoria has her heart set on finding a love like theirs," her Bachelor bio reads.

"Victoria is looking for a mature man who will be there for her in good times and bad. She is looking forward to meeting Zach because she knows he's as ready for lifelong love as much as she is!"

Viktoria E

Viktoria E is a 29-year-old Nanny from Vienna, Austria, who loves Christmas movies and lists to Norah Jones.

"Viktoria E. is a beautiful, positive woman whose striking looks are only a small part of her story. She was born and raised in Austria but moved to the United States by herself in 2013. Viktoria currently lives in New York City, where she loves being the life of the party and is always up for a new adventure," her Bachelor bio reads.
