When is Playboi Carti going on tour and how much do tickets cost?

JORDAN Terrell Carter, known professionally as Playboi Carti, has officially announced his North American Tour.

Carti's Narcissist tour will mark his first full headline tour since 2018 and includes over 40 shows.

When is Playboi Carti going on tour?

Carti announced his tour on September 1, 2021.

The Narcissist tour will kick off on October 14, 2021, and go through December 23.

How much are tickets to the Narcissist tour?

Presale tickets went on sale September 1 while general admission is scheduled to be released September 2, at 10am EST.

Tickets range from $49.50 to $99.50.

It is unknown if Carti plans on offering VIP packages for fans.

What album will Playboi Carti be performing on tour?

While he is expected to perform a variety of songs, the Narcissist tour is in support of his latest album "Whole Lotta Red."

Released December 25, 2020, the album was Carti's sixth release thought his career.
