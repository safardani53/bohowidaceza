Who is Robert Trujillo:

Robert Trujillo is a famous Bassist. He was born on October 23, 1964 and his birthplace is Santa Monica, CA. Robert is also well known as, Best known as the bassist for the rock band Metallica. He has also played with Infectious Grooves and Black Label Society. Alongside him in Metallica are singer/guitarist James Hetfield, drummer Lars Ulrich and guitarist Kirk Lee Hammett. 

Robert is originated from United States. He rerecorded bass tracks for the reissue of the Ozzy Osbourne album Blizzard of Ozz.
