Larissa Lima and Jess Caroline SLAM Colt Johnson for Revenge Porn: He Leaked Our Nudes!

Even though Jess Caroline dumped Colt Johnson a year ago, he’s not done with her — or with his ex-wife, Larissa.

Both exes accuse him of leaking their nudes in a deliberate act of revenge porn.

"Hello everyone," Jess Caroline shared this week on her Instagram Stories.

"I am really nervous right now about a DM I just received by a guy who says he is [Colt’s] friend," she expressed.

Jess reminds followers that Colt is "my ex boyfriend, a guy who uses women to stay relevant on the 90 Day Fiance show."

"He is the only person who I trusted to sending private pictures while we were dating long distance," Jess said.

"Now I just got this DM from his friend," she revealed, "and he told me [Colt] sent it to him."

Sharing someone’s nudes is a gross violation of their trust. It is also, in a growing number of places, a crime.

"Everyone already knows [Colt] loves to leak pictures of his [penis]," Jess pointed out.

"But I never could imagine he could get that far," she lamented, "and leak images that I trusted on him."

Jess is not the only ex of Colt’s to accuse him of this same exact crime.

"So hi guys, just an update," Larissa said in her Instagram Stories. "Thank you so much for all the info about the attorneys."

The video from her Stories, captured by 90 Day Fiance blogger John Yates, are included in this post.

"I don’t authorize him to use, in any degree, my photos, images on his OnlyFans account," Larissa clarifies.

"I don’t care what it is," Larissa emphasizes. "I just don’t want to be related to him."

"’Oh, Larissa, but you talk about him on the show,’" Larissa summarizes the way that her trolls will clap back at her over that line.

She reminds them: "The show was filmed a while ago." By our count, the latest that we saw of Colt and Jess was filmed about one year ago.

"And he sent my nudes to his girlfriend," Larissa laments, "[who] sent [them] to her husband."

She points out that revenge porn is a crime — which is true. Nevada specifically criminalizes the act.

Larissa says that her regular attorney — Adam, who is familiar to fans — is dealing with a family matter at this time and cannot help her.

In a follow-up (also included with this article), Larissa explains how she learned that Colt sent her nudes to his girlfriend.

She knows "because her husband came to me and was like …. ‘I have your photos.’"

Larissa also emphasizes how she wants to protect Jess Caroline from suffering this same indignity.

Larissa notes that she and Jess both send money back to their families in Brazil.

Now, they are having to spend that money on taking legal action against Colt — money that they could be sending home.

"The only way he’s gonna stop is be sued," Larissa says with resignation. "Let’s go. Let’s go by the court."

"Stop, grow, be a man," Larissa demands of Colt. "Stop with this s–t."

"You are a loser and you’re gonna die a loser because you’re never gonna grow," she predicts.

That is harsh … but it’s not an unrealistic outcome based upon the pattern of behavior that 90 Day Fiance fans and viewers have witnessed.
