Cherry Wallis Biography

Cherry Wallis is a famous British YouTuber known for her comedy, beauty, and fashion videos on her self-titled YouTube channel. Likewise, she is also popular for her work in The Darkest Dawn, Last Week, and Robot Wars.   

Short Biography & Early Life

Cherry Wallis was born on , in England, United Kingdom. She belongs to British nationality and has Caucasian ethnic descent. Likewise, her birth sign is Aries. 

Cherry was brought up in England alongside her brother Ben. She attended Art and Design college in Sutton Coldfield and specialized in photography there. Moreover, she graduated from Birmingham City University in BA(Hons) in Art and Design.

How Did She Become Famous?

Wallis began her YouTube journey on 8th June 2009 when she established her YouTube account as MsCherryGoesPop. Her first video is posted more than six years ago

Most of her content is related to beauty hacks, lifestyle, and comedy sketches. She rose to the spotlight after her video "Coke and Milk" went viral in September 2011. 

Similarly, she also won the 2016 Nickelodeon kid's choice awards for UK favorite Breakthrough Vlogger for her work.

As of September 2020, she has over 567k subscribers with 544 videos and over 62.946 million total views on her YouTube channel.

Some of her most viewed videos include Trying American candy, coke, and milk, My first Tattoo, Fantastic Beasts Case Unboxing, trying Japanese candy, and my harry potter collection.

On the other hand, her second channel named More Cherry has 12.9k subscribers as of 2020. The channel features her bloopers, song cover, and some extra content.

Besides YouTube, Cherry is also a singer. She released her debut song entitled Hero in and made it into the top 100 iTunes chart.
