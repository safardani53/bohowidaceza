Cardell’s Hummer rear-ended Will’s Mercedes, causing that vehicle to rear-end another car, driven by one of Will’s friends. A heated exchange broke out between the men following the collision, then Cardell pulled out a gun and shot Will, who died at the scene. He’s currently being held on $1 million bond in connection with the murder.
