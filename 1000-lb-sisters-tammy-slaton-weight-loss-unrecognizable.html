1000-lb Sisters' Tammy Slaton flaunts major weight loss in unrecognizable new pic in first Instagram

TAMMY Slaton from 1000-LB Sisters has shown off her dramatic weight loss in a new social media post.

The TLC reality star lost close to 200 pounds after facing major health challenges.

Tammy, 36, has now given fans another update on her weight loss journey.

The reality star reposted photos from her friend Joshua Douglas, which show off her smaller physique.

Tammy looks noticeably smaller posing next to her friend, who captioned the posts, "Literally the hottest girl in the world," and "Love you Tammy."

It's the first post on Tammy's account in more than a year, which truly shows off how much progress she's made.

WEIGHING IN

Throughout the last year, Tammy has lost a ton of weight, and it's completely changed her appearance.

In a recent episode clip of 1000-lb Sisters, Tammy weighed at her rehab facility to see if she met her goal weight.

The reality star has been hard at work trying to shed pounds in order to qualify for weight loss surgery.

In the video, Tammy said, "I need to get to 550 in order for me to be approved for surgery," as she gets out of her wheelchair and stepped on the scale.

"534.7," the nurse calls out.

"Tammy, you see that?!" Tammy's sister Amanda exclaimed as her other sister Misty complimented, "I'm just so proud of her."

In a confessional Tammy said, "I'm like 14 pounds under my goal weight, from over 700. That's a huge drop!"

When Tammy first arrived at the clinic, she weighed 717 pounds.

Realizing her accomplishment, Tammy exclaims: "I did that! I'm feeling thrilled. Proud. Excited. Just all the emotions.

"I proved everybody wrong. Everybody that doubted me," she said as she continued cheekily, "I finally got mines! My told-you-so moment!"

Amanda told her that the next time they see each other, Tammy will most likely be set up for her surgery.

She explained that while her sister still needs approval from other doctors, she was able to get past the first hurdle.

"I'm ready to get it over with," Tammy said.

By the end of the show's season three finale, Tammy had already lost 115 pounds in the rehab facility.

Before she started shedding the weight, Tammy hit rock bottom and had to be put on life support.

While discussing the terrifying situation in a recent episode, Tammy admitted that she "just didn't understand" why God had "saved" her life "again."

FINDING LOVE

Weight loss wasn't the only thing on Tammy's mind while in rehab. She also found herself a husband.

His name is Caleb Willingham, and he is in the same facility as the Slaton sister. The couple got married in November 2022 after getting engaged one month earlier.

The U.S. Sun exclusively shared the video of their engagement. Tammy said yes to Caleb's proposal at the parking lot of the rehab center.

Tammy announced her marriage to her fans with a video. She said: "Y'all knew me as Tammy Slaton, but now y'all know me as Mrs. Tammy Willingham."
