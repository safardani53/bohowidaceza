Tia Mowry Flashes Her Butt in a Sheer Dress: Red Carpet Photo

Can this red carpet get any hotter?  

So many sultry celebs turned up at the Focus premiere on Tuesday, Feb. 24, in Hollywood, that it’s almost impossible to keep up. While Christina Milian opted to show off her cleavage in a blue tuxedo sans bra and Margot Robbie was smoking in a monochromatic look, Tia Mowry was all about flaunting the backside.

“As you can see a lot of women are kind of rocking this look right now,” Mowry told Us Weekly, noting that the trend is inspired by Tom Ford and Givenchy. “But this is affordable.”  

The Instant Mom actress, 36, made a sexy strut for the movie starring Robbie and Will Smith, wearing a black Balmain blazer and a sheer dress by Stone Cold Fox with a NOE Undergarments bodysuit, and Stuart Weitzman sandals. Mowry’s lace dress costs $415, a steal compared to the high-end designer versions.
