Karl Lagerfeld on Pippa Middleton Face: Not a Fan!

Never one to mince words, krusty Karl Lagerfeld is at it again.

The rail-thin, 78-year-old fashion mogul caused outrage in February when he called Adele too fat. Now he’s firing shots at the lovely Pippa Middleton.

He’s not a fan of her face, apparently.

While Lagerfeld simply adores Prince William’s wife, the Duchess of Cambridge Kate Middleton, he is less than enamored by her younger sister.

He said recently: “Kate Middleton has a nice silhouette; she is the right girl for that boy. I like that kind of woman, I like romantic beauties.”

He should’ve stopped there, but added, “On the other hand, her sister struggles. I don’t like the sister’s face. She should only show her back.”

Aside from being just rude and harsh, the comment is surprising on superficial grounds. Pippa Middleton is very attractive, are we wrong?

You tell us: Does Pippa Middleton have a pretty face?
