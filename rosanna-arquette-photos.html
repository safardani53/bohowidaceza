See Photos Of The Actress Hollywood Life

Rosanna Arquette is part of a Hollywood dynasty. Known best for her BAFTA-winning role in 1985’s ‘Desperately Seeking Susan,’ Rosanna was one of the actresses that made the 1980s and early 1990s an incredible time for the movie industry. While she had roles in cult-classics like ‘Amazon Women on the Moon,’ ‘The Linguini Incident’ (featuring David Bowie!), and ‘After Hours,’ she found mainstream success with ‘New York Stories,’ the David Cronenberg ‘Crash,’ and ‘Pulp Fiction.’ In the 2000s and onward, she had success in numerous movies and television series, including a recurring role on ‘Ray Donavan,’ a main role on ‘What About Brian’ and a recurring role on the 2020 series, ‘Ratched.’

Rosanna is also the sister of Patricia Arquette (‘True Romance,’ ‘Boyhood,’ ‘Lost Highway’), Alexis Arquette (‘She’s All That,’ ‘Bride Of Chucky, ‘Here & Now’) and David Arquette (‘Scream,’ ‘Buffy the Vampire Slayer,’ ‘Eight Legged Freaks’).

Rosanna was involved with Toto keyboardist Steve Porcaro, inspiring the song ‘Rosanna.’ She was also married to Tony Greco, James Newton, John Sidel, and Todd Morgan.
