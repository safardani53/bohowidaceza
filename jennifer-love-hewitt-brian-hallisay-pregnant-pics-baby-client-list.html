[PICS] Jennifer Love-Hewitt &amp; Brian Hallisay Client List Stars Expecting Baby Hollywoo

Jennifer Love Hewitt and Brian Hallisay first met on the set of the Lifetime drama series ‘The Client List.’ Jennifer starred in the show as Riley Parks, while Brian played her husband, Kyle Parks. A real-life romance formed between the two, and they married on November 20, 2013. That same month, they welcomed their first child, daughter Autumn, and welcomed their second child, son Atticus, in June 2015. In Sept. 2020, Jennifer and Brian became parents for a third time when their son Aidan was born. 

While in the public eye, Jennifer and Brian keep a pretty low profile. Jennifer has been starring on the popular Fox drama ‘9-1-1.’ Brian also appeared on the series in a recurring role, and most recently appeared in an episode of the final season of ‘Suits.’ To see photos of the happy couple together over the years, click through the gallery.
