CORONATION Street viewers think Michelle Connor is working with Vicky Jefferies to frame Robert Preston for murder.

The Bistro owner - who is played by actress Kym Marsh in the ITV soap - tricked Robert into signing over the Bistro to her to stop Vicky from making a claim on it.

Pregnant Vicky crashed the couple's engagement party and made a big deal about Robert scaring her.

First she did it inside before he had even touched her and outside when he tried to get her out of the road she acted as if she was terrified he would kill her.

She told him she was going to take him for every penny he's worth for their baby, leading to Robert panicking about losing everything he worked for.

When he confided in Michelle she suggested he sign over the business to her to protect it from Vicky.

He panicked: "Legally, as soon as the baby's born she's going to apply for child support.

"I've always said I would see her right and take care of the baby. But now she's not having it.

"She wants to make some sort of claim against everything I own, everything that I earn, she wants to take me to the cleaners Michelle, she wants to ruin me.

"Everything I've got, everything I've built up."

She told him: "Well hey, tell you what then, sign it over to me. If the Bistro's in my name, she can't touch it at all, can she?"

Michelle later had a meltdown over Vicky crashing the wedding and convinced Robert to go over to confront her leading to the pair having a furious row with Vicky screaming at the top of her lungs.

She hurled mugs at him and caused a huge row that the concerned neighbours overheard.

It has fans convinced Michelle and Vicky are working together to take Robert down.
