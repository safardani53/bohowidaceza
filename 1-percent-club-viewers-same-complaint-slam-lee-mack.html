The 1% Club viewers all have the same complaint as they slam Lee Mack game show

VIEWERS of Lee Mack's game show The 1% Club all had the same complaint about the show's latest episode.

The ITV game show beings with 100 contestants, who are then whittled down as they compete to make it to the end.

The final goal is to answer a question only 1% of the country can get right, in hopes of winning up to 100,000 pounds.

But fans of the show soon took to social media to complain that the questions in this week's episode were far too easy.

ITV are clearly thrilled with the show's success as they have already ordered a second series.

He presents the challenge series, which sees celebs compete in a series of icy endurance tasks, with Holly Willoughby.
