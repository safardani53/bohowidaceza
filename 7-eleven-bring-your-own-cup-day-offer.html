7-Elevens Bring Your Own Cup day offers customers iconic frozen Slurpee in cup as big as a bucket

7 ELEVEN’s iconic holiday has returned, providing customers the chance to bring any size container to get as much Slurpee as they want.

On April 29, all participating 7-Eleven, Speedway, and Stripes locations will allow customers to bring any “cup” that fits their dispensers to get as much Slurpee as they want for $2. 

The store explained how the holiday works, sharing that there’s a limited-time-only flavor available: the Fanta Dragon Fruit Zero Sugar flavor. 

This drink will accompany the store’s classics like Coca-Cola, Cherry, and Blue Raspberry.

"We love seeing customers enjoy our products in fun ways – especially when it comes to our beloved, iconic Slurpee drink," said 7-Eleven.

"From cowboy boot to fishbowl...the more creative the cup, the better.

“We can't wait to see what our customers come up with this year."

Despite the loose approach of the store, some rules have been established.

Rules to follow

  • The container must be “food-safe” and clean.
  • The container must fit “upright” in the 10-inch hole that the store has on display and must also fit under the Slurpee dispenser.
  • The container must be watertight. 

The store also has a solution for people who forget to bring their own cups.

“7-Eleven is also offering $1 small Slurpee drinks via the 7NOW® delivery app, so customers can still get their sip on in style,” says 7-Eleven.

Despite the rules established by the company, it’s ultimately up to staff on location to determine the parameters of the containers that are accepted. 

At the end of the day, as long as the container is clean and of a relatively moderate size, customers should be good to go. 

Ideas as crazy as sleds, plant pots without draining holes, and more, have been used in 7-Eleven stores. 

In short, get ready for some wild sights when looking for a Slurpee today.
