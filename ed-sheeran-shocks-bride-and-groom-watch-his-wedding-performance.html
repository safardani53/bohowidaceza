Ed Sheeran SHOCKS Bride and Groom: Watch His Wedding Performance!

After splitting from Athina Andrelos, Ed Sheeran may be single.

But that doesn’t mean the singer isn’t in the mood for love.

In an amazing gesture organized by Australian radio show Kylie and Jackie O, the 24-year old artist recently surprised Kya and Matt Debono on their wedding day.

In footage posted online of the nuptials, the newlyweds are getting ready for their first dance when the radio hosts walk in and announce the following:

"It is time for our first dance, and that is a very memorable moment at a wedding. So we wanted to make your first dance a little bit more special today…

"We’re going to leave you with a little wedding singer we organized and we hope you enjoy, everyone."

And enjoy they sure did, as Taylor Swift’s best friend took to the stage and serenaded the happy couple with his hit single "Thinking Out Loud."

Sheeran proceeded to share a cute picture of himself posing with the couple on Instagram, writing:

"Just surprised this lovely couples first dance. Available for weddings, birthdays and bar mitzvah’s, contact your local super market for details."

HA! We love it. And we love Ed Sheeran.
