Pics Of Kendall Jenner &amp; More Hollywood Life

Little black dresses are one of the most important fashion staples to have in your closet. From super casual to dressy, there’s an LBD for just about every occasion, and stars love rocking all the different variations. When in doubt, our favorite celebrities love choosing a little black dress for the red carpet, a night out, and other public occasions.

In 2017, Selena Gomez attended the InStyle Awards, and had the most perfect LBD for the red carpet. The dress was fairly plain and simple, with thin straps and a low-cut neckline, but it showed off Sel’s legs to perfection. She completed the look with nude-colored heels and her hair pulled back into a sleek updo. There was also a dramatic eye makeup element to go with the fierce ensemble.

Ariel Winter loves a good mini dress, so we’ve seen her rock the LBD look quite often over the years. At one event, she paired her tiny dress with a black robe, which she tied around her midsection and let fall off one shoulder. The addition brought a sexy bedroom vibe to the otherwise simple look. Ariel paired the dress with clear heels, which were held to her feet with black straps around the ankles.

Meanwhile, Sofia Richie showed us how to rock a more casual little black dress while out and about in California. Her ensemble was less dressy than Ariel or Sofia’s, and she wore it for a day of running errands. She paired the outfit with closed-toed shoes and sunglasses for an effortless look that was perfect for her daytime outing.

As for Larsa Pippen — she enjoyed a night out on the town in her black mini. Larsa’s strapless dress was skintight and paired with pointy-toed heels. She completed the ensemble with a diamond necklace, as well. Check out the gallery above for photos of more stars wearing this hot trend that never goes out of style!
