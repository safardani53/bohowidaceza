Virgin Media TV subscribers can access a huge new upgrade - but there's a catch

SPORTS fans on Virgin Media are in for a telly treat.

Customers can finally access Sky Sports in beautiful Ultra HD.

That means crystal clear Premier League, cricket, darts and more.

TV subscribers can add Sky Sports Ultra HD to their plan for Â£7 per month.

It includes Sky Sports Main Event Ultra HD and Sky Sports Ultra HD 1.

All existing Virgin TV 360 and V6 customers can add it onto their account.

Sky Cinema and Entertainment Ultra HD VOD are already available via Virgin Media.

The move follows a similar upgrade for other Sky channels.

Depending on the package you have, you could get up to 14 channels in HD for the first time.

National Geographic HD, Sky Comedy HD and Sky Arts HD are among them.
