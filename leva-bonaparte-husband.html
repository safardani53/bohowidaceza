Who Is Leva Bonaparte's Husband? Meet Her Spouse

Who is Leva Bonaparte's husband? She joined the cast 'Southern Charm' for Season 7 and is now starring in a spinoff called 'Southern Hospitality.'

On the heels of Southern Charm Season 8 comes a new spinoff featuring Leva Bonaparte. It's called Southern Hospitality, and it kicked off on Bravo on Nov. 28, 2022. The reality TV series follows Leva as she manages a nightclub called Republic Garden & Lounge, which she owns with her husband. But who is her business partner and partner in life? Let's take a closer look.

Who is Leva Bonaparte's husband?

Leva joined Southern Charm Season 7 as full-time cast member, but she had been friends with much of the cast for years. (She even made cameo appearances on the show before officially joining.) Although she returned for Season 8, Bravo fans soon learned that she was granted her own spinoff.

Because her husband doesn't play a major role in the series, folks are understandably curious to learn more about him.

Lena has been married to Lamar Bonaparte since April of 2013. Lamar is a Charleston native, and he attended South Carolina State University. He received a degree in accounting from there.

Lamar has been working in the hospitality industry for more than 20 years.

What is Leva Bonaparte's job?

In September 2019, they opened Mesu, a Mexican and sushi fusion restaurant. Leva and Lamar also own Republic Garden & Lounge, and Bourbon N' Bubbles. All of their establishments are on King Street, which is one of Charleston's most recognizable and famous streets.

In 2020, they also opened 1st Place Pub Premium Sports Pub, also located on King Street.

Southern Hospitality will mostly feature Leva managing Republic Garden & Lounge and its staff.

What is Leva Bonaparte's ethnicity?

The Persian beauty was born in India, but she was raised in both Canada and Bolivia. Her family eventually moved to Charleston, S.C. for business purposes, and Leva ultimately stayed.
