Kirsten Storms Arrested for DUI

Shortly after 3 a.m. Friday, authorities stopped soap opera actress Kirsten Storms along the 101 Freeway in Studio City after they say they saw her toss a lit cigarette out the window of her Mercedes.

Officers then issued sobriety tests, which the former Days of Our Lives and current General Hospital star failed, says the CHP, which declined to state her blood-alcohol level.

Storms, who also plays Bonnie Rockwaller on Disney’s Kim Possible was booked at the Los Angeles jail in Van Nuys at 4:26 a.m.

The actress was released eight hours later on $5,000 bail, pending an October 2 court appearance in Van Nuys, according to the Los Angeles Sheriff’s Department.

Rumor has it that Lindsay Lohan sent Storms a bouquet of flowers.
