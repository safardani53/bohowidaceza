Coronation Streets Lucy Fallon is dating Preston footballer Ryan Ledson after split from fiance T

CORONATION Street's Lucy Fallon is dating Preston footballer Ryan Ledson, The Sun can reveal.

The 25-year-old star split from her fiance Tom Leech for a second time last year - and has now moved on with a new man.

The actress is now blissfully loved up with hunky Ryan after meeting him through former Corrie co-star Helen Flanagan's footballer partner.

A source told The Sun: "It's early days but they're really loved up and don't care who knows it.

"They met through Helen Flanagan's partner Scott Sinclair, who knows Ryan."

The insider went on: "Everyone wants Lucy to be happy after the rollercoaster of her last year - leaving Corrie, and splitting with Tom."

Lucy's rep had no comment when approached by The Sun.

She and Ryan have made little secret of their growing romance - leaving loved-up comments on each other's Instagram accounts, including tell-tale heart emojis.

The beauty also hinted that she had a boyfriend in a cryptic Instagram post last month, where she joked: "When your boyfriend is worried about you at the club but this is you at the club x".

Back in January, The Sun revealed that Lucy had secretly reunited with fiance Tom before dumping him for a second time.

She'd initially ended their relationship shortly before filming ITV's Don't Rock The Boat last summer.

Pals later told us that she gave their three-year romance one last try after filming finished - before kicking him out of their shared house in Blackpool for a final time.
