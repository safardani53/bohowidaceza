Party Down Season 3 Is Set To Released On The Starz Network

After more than a decade, the renowned American comedy Party Down is ready to return with a fresh new third season. Season 3 will premiere on the Starz Network on Friday, February 24, 2023, at 9 p.m. ET/PT. In addition to being acknowledged as the show’s creators, Rob Thomas, John Enbom, Paul Rudd, and Dan Etheridge have acted as executive producers for the Starz series. Furthermore, Adam Scott, Jennifer Dugan, and Nancy van Doornewaard create Party Down.

Season 3 of Starz’s Party Down will consist of six episodes.

The official summary, trailer, and predictions for the next season

The first episode of Party Down’s third season will premiere on Starz TV Channel on Friday, February 24, 2023. The new episode will run at 9 p.m. ET/PT. The official summary for the sitcom’s third season, as released by Starz Channel, reads:

“Ten years after failing actor Henry Pollard gave up playing and his day job working for Party Down Catering, a run of bad luck puts him back with his old band of Hollywood dreamers and misfits tending bar.”

Season 3 will start a decade after the events of Season 2 of Party Down. The new season seems to feature a slew of incredibly amusing new experiences, as well as fan-favorite party caterers. Starz released the official teaser on January 26, 2023, offering viewers a peek at what is to come in the next season. The spectator can see in the teaser that the beloved catering crew is reuniting and is back in business.

As the gang navigates its way into the next chapter of its life, the newest season will take spectators on an emotionally charged and hilariously constructed rollercoaster ride.

How many episodes will there be in the upcoming season?

Season 3 will consist of six episodes. The following is a list of all the episodes and their release dates:

  • Episode 1: Kyle Bradway Is Nitromancer – February 24, 2023
  • Episode 2: Jack Botty’s Delayed Post-Pandemic Surprise Party – March 3, 2023
  • Episode 3: First Annual PI2A Symposium – March 10, 2023
  • Episode 4: KSGY-95 Prizewinner’s Luau – March 17, 2023
  • Episode 5: Once Upon a Time Proms Away Prom-otional Event – March 24, 2023
  • Episode 6: Sepulveda Basin High School Spring Play Opening Night – March 31, 2023

List of Season 3 cast members

The main cast list for the American sitcom’s latest season includes:

  • Adam Scott as Henry Pollard
  • Jane Lynch as Constance Carmell
  • Ryan Hansen as Kyle Bradway
  • Ken Marino as Ronald Wayne “Ron” Donald
  • Megan Mullally as Lydia Dunfree
  • Martin Starr as Roman DeBeers
