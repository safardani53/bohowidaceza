First look inside Kendall Jenners new 5.7m six bedroom mansion in West Hollywood The Sun

Kendall Jenner is building up quite the property portfolio.

After splashing out on an LA condo in 2014, the supermodel has just forked out £5.7m ($7.5m USD) on a sleek mansion in the Hollywood Hills.

The plush six bedroom home was previously owned by Emily Blunt and her husband John Krasinski and the property boasts plenty of space - in fact there's nearly 5,000 square feet of it.

Kendall and her girl squad are free to enjoy the huge swimming pool area, sunbathe on the roof terrace or drink cocktails in the bar area.

The home also boasts a luxury master bed with a large walk in wardrobe to house all of Kendall's designer wares.
