Lucy Fallon And Ryan Ledson Celebrating Valentines Day Together

Lucy Fallon, a Coronation Street actress, went out with her boyfriend Ryan Ledson to celebrate Valentine’s Day. Fallon initially shared a mirror selfie on her Instagram Stories while wearing a flowery outfit. The tale included an upside-down happy face and the caption “Date nite w bbe.” She followed up with another post from her date night with Ledson a few hours later. She shared a photo of herself and her partner drinking at a nice restaurant. “Thank you for constantly singing ‘All Too Well – Taylor Swift’ in the vehicle with me,” she captioned the photo, which was accompanied by multiple cupid love emojis. “Every day is Valentine’s Day, darling x.”

Lucy Fallon is dating Ryan Ledson.

According to The Sun, Fallon met her fiance via former Corrie co-star Helen Flanagan’s partner, Scott Sinclair. She subsequently claimed that they met on the dating app Raya. The publication also shared multiple images of Fallon and Ledson talking on Instagram.

Rumors of a Breakup

Several speculations circulated in December 2021 claiming that Fallon and Ledson had called it quits. The suspicions surfaced when her Raya profile was revealed. “Lucy seems to have handed Ryan the red card because she has a profile on Raya,” a source told The Sun. The split rumor gained a lot of attention, and Fallon personally put a stop to it. “I’m in such a joyful mood so was going to ignore this but to keep the festivities alive, I’m not,” she posted on Instagram Stories. “Ryan and I met last year on Raya. I no longer need Raya since I now have Ryan. And I’m happier than I’ve ever been. Enjoy the holiday’s everyone… but not if you work for the Sun. You’re welcome to a terrible one, xxx.”

Who Is Ryan Ledson?

Ledson is a Preston North End professional soccer player who plays as a midfielder. He joined Everton after graduating from the Toffees’ academy. According to his club’s website, Ledson joined Cambridge on loan before making the permanent move to Oxford. Prior to departing Oxford, he was named Player of the Year, Young Player of the Year, and Goal of the Season at the completion of the 2017/18 season. Ledson signed a three-year agreement with Preston North End in the summer of 2018 for an unknown price. He was also selected for the 2020-21 season’s Players and Supporters Players of the Year.
