My boyfriend and I planned a Dollar Tree date night to suit our budget - we used a game to make it e

A COUPLE planned a Dollar Tree date where they played a romantic and budget-friendly game to save some cash.

The Florida couple inspired TikTok users with their video which nearly has one million views.

The original poster shared the video under the name Dylan Owens and captioned it, "Dollar Tree Date Night bc we're both poor AND IT WAS THE (bomb emoji.)"

The TikTok shows Owens playing rock paper scissors with his girlfriend in a Dollar Tree to determine who buys each course for their romantic meal.

The discount chain stores have become a staple for the American public as a 2020 survey revealed that over half of Americans shop at dollar stores.

Owens won the first round and secured some soda to enjoy with their meal.

Dollar Stores' most popular item

This comes as a Dollar Store employee revealed their most popular item and why they warn customers to never buy it.

To determine what Americans are buying at dollar stores, GoBankingRates conducted a survey of over 1,000 American adults in April.

The survey revealed that 24 percent of dollar store patrons buy food, followed closely by personal care items at 22 percent.

Party supplies and home goods ranked highly as well, pulling in 17 percent and 15 percent of the vote.

However, despite it being the most popular item at discount stores, a Dollar Tree employee recently warned customers not to buy frozen seafood or meat while shopping at the locations.

The employee, known as Brenda, has admitted that some of the products can be hit and miss.

She said that the store’s cinnamon bites are “amazing” and admitted customers love the frozen fruit and veg, according to Mental Floss.

But, she warned: “I don’t eat any of the frozen fish or rib eyes because I don’t trust frozen seafood or meat that costs a dollar.”
