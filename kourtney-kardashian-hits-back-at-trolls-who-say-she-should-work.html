Kourtney Kardashian Hits back at Trolls Who Say She Should Work More, Travel Less

It’s 2019 and the Kardashians rule the world. 

So, If you’re one of those people who still feel the need to ask "but, like, what do they even do?" please take my little piece of advice; don’t. 

Besides building their empire, makeup brands, filming a whole TV show, freeing prisoners from jail (& more!), the Kardashians are also experts at having to defend themselves to the public.

This time around, Kourtney Kardashian is stepping up to the plate after an Instagram user claimed that all she does is vacation and that she doesn’t actually work.

On August 18, Kourt shared a series of photos from her latest golf getaway to Idaho with her children Mason, Penelope, and Reign Disick. 

While the post was harmless and fun, some internet trolls didn’t find it too amusing, commenting, "Kourtney this is why ppl say you don’t work girl." 

"Excuse me", is all we can imagine Kourtney saying in her super monotone voice after reading that harsh reply. She also probably laughed because she has a good sense of humor. 

But the mother of three quickly fired back, saying, "We all have our priorities."

"So I’ll be making memories with my kids and amazing people while living my life to the absolute fullest."

And just to drive the message home that she does work, since everyone seems to be super concerned about that, she added "Travel diary coming soon @Poosh," with a laughing emoji. 

But that wasn’t all Kourt had to say about her controversial work ethic. 

Another IG user thanked her for being a "dedicated mom", which led a separate person to say, "I’m a dedicated mom, I think working moms are dedicated too ya know."

Uh yes, she knows! 

"Who says I am not a working mom? replied Kourtney. "Because I most definitely am."

"Working moms can be dedicated, of course. As women, we should be supporting each other for what allows us to be our best, not being so critical or judgmental of each other."

"We all do things our own way," she concluded.

Can I just remind you that all of these unnecessary and petty stabs stemmed from a golf trip… in Idaho.

No offense to Idaho, but I think Kourt’s recent trips to Italy or Costa Rica (with Scott!) would’ve served as a better time for these women to share their thoughts. We would 100% understand their jealousy then, because same. 

But everyone can calm down because her adorable kiddos will be starting school here soon, so the vacation sprees will probably start slowing down. 

Either way, just because most of us can’t afford or take the time to travel the world, doesn’t mean kourt shouldn’t. 

In fact, she should because that is exactly what contributes to her ~career~ and you know we will all be watching her lavish vacays play out here soon, whether it’s from Poosh or on E!. 

She’s living her best life. And yes, we’re living ours vicariously. It’s fine.
