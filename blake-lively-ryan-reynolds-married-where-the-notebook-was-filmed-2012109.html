Blake Lively, Ryan Reynolds Married Where The Notebook Was Filmed!

Most romantic wedding venue ever?

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds married Sunday at Boone Hall Plantation in Mount Pleasant, South Carolina — the same place where the The Notebook was filmed in 2004!

A source confirms to Us Weekly that Lively, 25, and Reynolds, 35, who began dating in October 2011, said “I do” in a surprise, private ceremony at the stunning property familiar to fans of the 2004 tearjerker starring Ryan Gosling and Rachel McAdams, based on Nicholas Sparks‘ wildly popular romance novel.

Boone Hall Plantation is one of America’s oldest working plantations and has been open to the public since 1956. In the film, the plantation served as Allie’s (McAdams) family’s summer home — where she met and fell in love with Noah (Gosling). Lively briefly dated Gosling in December 2010, shortly after the Gossip Girl actress split from boyfriend/costar Penn Badgley of three years.
