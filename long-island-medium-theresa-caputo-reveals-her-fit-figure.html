BIG B-DAY

The reality TV star took to Instagram on Saturday with a photo from her actual birthday, which is June 10.

In the image, Theresa stood between her daughter Victoria, 28, and son-in-law Michael Mastrandrea.

Victoria held her baby daughter, Michelina, who was born in February last year.

The group was all dressed to the nines, with Victoria in a floral yellow maxi dress, Michael in a light blue linen suit, and little Michelina in a white tulle gown paired with colorful sneakers.

Behind the group, a beautiful scene of Italy could be seen, with rose bushes, green mountains, and calm water.

Standing in the middle of her family, Theresa celebrated turning 56 in style.

She wore a stunning silver gown with black stripes, which fitted tightly to her chest and waist before falling down to the floor with a huge thigh slit.

She added a pair of eye-catching high heels with silver bows, and styled her hair in a voluminous beehive.

Theresa captioned the shot: "Birthday celebration Italian style. La Mia Famiglia."
