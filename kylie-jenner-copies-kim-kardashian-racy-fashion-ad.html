KYLIE Jenner has copied sister Kim Kardashian with a very racy new Dolce & Gabbana ad after Kim's weight loss.

Kylie took note of Kim's Dolce & Gabbana ad that was released last month for her own ad campaign with the fashion brand.

She also stunned in black and white sunglasses with the brand name on the sides.

The Hulu star dons a sparkly silver skirt, matched with a breast-bearing crop top and crystal cross necklace.

KIM'S WEIGHT LOSS

Throughout last year, fans repeatedly expressed concern about Kim, and her slimming frame, especially after learning about some of her more unconventional methods for losing weight.

Kim was known for her signature curves – specifically her butt – but last year, the reality star seemed determined to have a more slender physique.

While the TV personality has documented her fitness journey for years, she kicked things into high gear and started to shed some serious weight.

In October last year, Kim made her Saturday Night Live.

While her monologue and her sketch appearances generated plenty of buzz, fans couldn't help but focus on how much thinner she looked.

She continued slimming down in the following months, documenting her workouts and diets via her Instagram Stories and more.

In May this year, Kim hit the red carpet at the Met Gala alongside then-boyfriend Pete Davidson and the rest of her famous family, who were invited for the first time.

She stunned in a gown originally worn by Marilyn Monroe as she famously sang happy birthday to John F. Kennedy, but it was how she managed to fit into the dress that sent fans' tongues wagging.

The Skims founder told Vogue that to get the gown on, she had to go on an extreme diet that required her to eliminate many foods from her diet.

She told the magazine: "I tried it on and it didn't fit me. I said, 'Give me three weeks.' 

"I had to lose 16 pounds, down today."

Kim went on: "It was such a challenge. It was like a [movie] role. I was determined to fit [into] it. I haven't had carbs or sugar in about three weeks. 

Kardashian fans were shocked, slamming Kim's diet as unhealthy.

But Kim continued slimming down, showing off her new, significantly less curvy figure on social media and at events.

KYLIE'S FITNESS JOURNEY

After Kylie split from Travis Scott, she has been focusing on herself and her fitness. She also shut down critics who accused her of taking diet pills.

Some of the Kardashian-Jenner critics don't believe that Kylie got her banging body through legitimate means.

Kylie, like some of her sisters, has been accused of taking diet pills to maintain her slim figure.

To clap back at her haters, the Hulu star made a prominent change to her Instagram profile by adding one Instagram highlight.

The highlight is labeled with an emoji of a person lifting a weight over their head, and it's filled with photos and videos of Kylie's workouts from the past two years.

The highlight also includes videos of her wearing a waist trainer and photos of her expensive home workout equipment.

There are several videos of Kylie running, using the Stairmaster, and working out on the pilates machine.
