Apple's Airpods Pro are available at their lowest EVER price - saving you 57

APPLE’S Airpods Pro are available at a new low price right now.

The more expensive of Apple’s earbuds are 23% off at Onbuy.com – a saving of £57.

The incredible price means that you can pick up the Airpods Pro new for just £191.99. It’s still a sizeable price tag of course, but it’s the first time we’ve spotted the Pros below £200.

In fact, at £57 off the regular price, Onbuy’s listing looks to be the lowest ever price for Apple’s top-tier model.

While the discount doesn't appear to be time-limited, we can’t be sure how long it will be around. Onbuy is an online marketplace for sellers, so orders will be fulfilled by sellers depending on stock.

Because of this, it is also best to check seller details, terms and returns policies before you commit to any purchase.

  • Airpods Pro, £191, at Onbuy.com - buy here

In terms of price Onbuy’s listing , it's such great value because Apple’s current pricing of the Airpods Pro is £249.

The cheapest price we've previously seen for the Airpods Pro is £205 at Laptops Direct.

Meanwhile Amazon, usually among the lowest for tech devices, has previously dropped the price of the Pros to £204.50. But they are currently listed at £215.

That makes Onbuy’s listing the cheapest yet with free delivery as a bonus. But, as with returns, delivery is provided by the seller and not Onbuy itself.

At the time of writing, the primary seller of the Airpods Pro states free delivery takes 4-6 days. So it’s worth bearing that in mind if you want a quicker arrival.

Are the Airpods Pro worth it? The Sun reviewed the the earbuds back in November 2019, and called them “easy – to set up, to use and to love”.

Tech and Science Editor, Sean Keach, stated: “I have zero complaints about the AirPods so far, except for the fact that I'm probably wearing them so much that people around me think I hate them.”

“But I don't care! I can't hear their complaints thanks to that sweet, sweet noise-cancellation. Ahh.”
