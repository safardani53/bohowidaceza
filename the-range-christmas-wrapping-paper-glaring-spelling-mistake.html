The Range fans left 'fuming' at Christmas wrapping paper as shoppers spot glaring spelling mistake

THE Range fans are left ‘fuming’ at Christmas wrapping paper, as shoppers have spotted a glaring spelling mistake - and it’s over the entire roll. 

So if you bought your gift wrap from The Range this year, you might want to give it a once over before wrapping up your gifts.

One angry high-street shopper took to social media to share the awkward festive blunder, leaving many open-mouthed.

The brown gift wrap is covered with images of Santa Claus, presents, snowflakes, snowmen and penguins.

But each Santa Claus (and there’s many) is holding up a cheery sign.

However, instead of the sign reading Merry Christmas, it has been spelt incorrectly and actually says ‘Meery Christmas’.

Attempting to make light of the situation, the user joked: “Meery Christmas to you too!”

The TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @hjb83_1, has clearly left many stunned, as it has quickly amassed a whopping 97,200 views in just 15 hours.

Social media users were left in hysterics at the awkward blunder and many took to the comments to express this. 

At the same time, many users claimed that it took them a while to figure out the error. 

One person said: “Omg, it took me a minute to see what you was on about.”

Another added: “Ngl that took me a while, even more embarrassing!”

Meanwhile, other users tried to reassure the person that the blunder was barely noticeable. 

One user penned: “Fuming? No one would notice.”

A second chimed in: “No one is gonna notice, no one pays attention to wrapping paper.”

Whilst someone else claimed: “Like any kids gona notice.”

Stunned at the fail, one user shared: “That went through design, production, quality checking and packaging.”

Meanwhile, a savvy person suggested: “If you have elves, blame it on them.”
