Tattoo artist shows off freckles she inked on a client but people are horrified

AN AUSSIE tattoo artist has gone viral online after showing off one of her latest creations - inked freckles - but viewers are not the biggest fans.

Daisy Lovesick from Brisbane, Australia, revealed one of her most recent portfolio additions - tattooed freckles - but people have been left open-mouthed.

According to the artist, the client, Michaela, travelled 900 miles just to get their face covered in inked freckles, with a mini heart-shaped freckle too.

Demonstrating what the woman looked like prior to the appointment, Daisy said they had agreed to get medium coverage freckles.

She also revealed: ''I always map my freckles with my clients sitting up and I have them go and look in the mirror to check that they're happy before we get started.

''When I map for a freckle tattoo, I map intuitively by eye,'' Daisy (@daisylovesick), explained the process in another video.
