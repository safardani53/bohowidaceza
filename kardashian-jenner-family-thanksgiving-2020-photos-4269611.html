Kardashian &amp; Jenner Family Celebrates Thanksgiving 2020 Photos Hollywood Life

Like most holidays, the Kardashians and Jenners usually go all out. They get their massive family together to eat, drink, and be merry. Due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, things will be a little different this year since California’s restrictions limit gatherings to no more than 3 separate households.

Despite not being able to be all together this year for Thanksgiving, the KarJenners have a lot to be thankful for. Kourtney Kardashian, 41, and Scott Disick, 37, started off Thanksgiving by showing their thanks for their adorable kiddos. Kylie Jenner, 23, and Khloe Kardashian, 36, soon followed and gave fans an inside look at the Thanksgiving celebrations for the KarJenners this year. Kim Kardashian, 40, was noticeably absent from their snaps, so maybe she just had a private Thanksgiving this year.

Kylie Jenner

Kylie Jenner looked gorgeous in a simple black sweater and her long blonde hair styled in loose waves. She also posted a gorgeous selfie with mom Kris Jenner, 65, and they promoted the Black Friday sale for Kylie Skin and Kylie Cosmetics.

Kylie raved over the KarJenner Thanksgiving spread, including the honey bar and Khloe Kardashian’s Oreo cake. She trolled Kourtney for only bringing chips and dip to Thanksgiving. The KarJenners who got together ate with turkey-embellished plates surrounded by stunning flowers.

Khloe Kardashian

Khloe Kardashian put in work on Thanksgiving. She revealed on her Instagram Story that she made Oreo cupcakes and Oreo cake for the family get-together. She also got a “divine” recipe from Kim Zolciak-Biermann for monkey bread. Later, she gushed that she was “nail twins” with Kylie as they sported red and green holiday-themed nails.

Khloe also shared an adorable video of True Thompson, 2, and Stormi Webster, 2, running around on Thanksgiving. They were eating ice cream and giggling up a storm. Stormi looked adorable in a white dress, while True rocked a white shirt and a denim and plaid skirt.

Kourtney Kardashian

Kourtney Kardashian began gushing over what she is thankful for in the earlier hours of Thanksgiving. She posted a hilarious throwback video — you can tell by Reign’s long hair! — that featured Scott Disick and their kids: Mason, 10, Penelope, 8, and Reign, 5. Kourtney had all the kids laughing up a storm as she used a baby filter on all of them.

When she panned to Scott, he started acting like he was Mason and told everyone to “subscribe to my YouTube channel.” The kids couldn’t stop laughing at their dad. Kourtney simply captioned the Instagram video, “Thankful.”

Later, Kourtney posted a breathtaking photo of mom Kris at their Thanksgiving table. Kris rocked black latex pants and a sleek black turtleneck. She was also holding a martini glass in her hand!

Scott Disick

Scott Disick kicked off this year’s Thanksgiving by sharing photos of all three of his kids with Kourtney on his Instagram Story. He captioned a goofy photo with Reign that he was “very thankful.” He posted a sweet photo of Penelope sleeping and wrote that he was “very thankful for my kiddies.”
