BBC star Aimee Fuller turns heads in see-through dress as she brings the glam to 2023 SPOTY awards

AIMEE FULLER dressed to impress at this year's Sports Personality of the Year award.

The BBC host dazzled in a sparkly outfit as she strutted down the red carpet.

Aimee accompanied her dress with huge bow earrings and stylish high-heeled shoes.

The 32-year-old had a great time a she put on a leggy display alongside ex-cricketer pal Ebony Rainford-Brent.

And she was delighted to see young snowboarder Mia Brookes pick up the Young Sports Personality of the Year gong.

She was handed the award for becoming the youngest ever snowboarding world champion just a month after her 16th birthday.

The blonde bombshell was full of praise for Brookes, tweeting: "Deserved. The future is bright."

Aimee represented Great Britain in the 2014 and 2018 Winter Olympics.

Her performances in the slopestyle snowboarding event saw her finish 17th at both Games.

Fuller has now swapped the piste for the much warmer BBC studio, leading coverage and commentating on the action from the slopes at the 2022 Winter Olympics.
