Photos Of The Sexy Looks Hollywood Life

The Kardashian and Jenner sisters are always up with the hottest trends, and they’ve given a new name to thigh-high boots over the last few years. High boots go great when paired with mini dresses, skirts and shorts, as they provide some coverage while still making an outfit look super sexy. This look comes in all different styles and colors, and the KarJenner girls have done it all!

Kim Kardashian and Kylie Jenner showed off two very different ways to rock high boots when they visited the Yeezy offices one time. Kim was dressed down for the occasion in a pair of bike shorts and a windbreaker, but she used her grey boots to spice up the look. Meanwhile, Kylie was in a much more dressy ensemble for the occasion, as she rocked a skintight mini dress. The outfit was paired with thigh-high blue boots, which still kept a sliver of skin revealed on her thighs.

Kourtney Kardashian wore thigh-high boots for a night out on the town, and really dressed up her otherwise simple outfit with the shoes. Kourt rocked a vintage Metallica t-shirt as a dress for the occasion, and she wore bright blue boots to go with it. The boots elevated the look from pajamas — to high fashion! The hot mama completed her look with very natural makeup, putting her glowing skin on display. She also wore her hair slicked back into a high ponytail.

Meanwhile, Khloe wore the most extreme thigh-high boot look of all when went to lunch by the beach in Malibu. She paired her tight black leggings and snakeskin jacket with boots that nearly reached hip-height. The shoes had a long, thin heel, but Khloe walked in them like a pro.
