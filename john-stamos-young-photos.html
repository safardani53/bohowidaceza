Photos From His Young Days to Now Hollywood Life

John Stamos, born August 19, 1963, is one of America’s most beloved hunks, winning over the hearts of generations of TV viewers. John began his career on daytime TV before landing the part of Uncle Jesse on Full House in 1987. He would reprise the role in 2016 with the Netflix reboot, Fuller House, which he also executive produced.

John’s other TV hits include the NBC medical drama ER, the Fox sitcom Grandfathered, the horror-comedy Scream Queens, and the Lifetime/Netflix psychological thriller You (2018). John hasn’t limited his charm to the small screen. He’s also appeared in the Broadway revival of Bye Bye Birdie and of Gore Vidal’s play The Best Man.
