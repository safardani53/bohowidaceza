TOWIE stars Tommy Mallet and Georgia Kousoulou celebrated their marriage with a Chinese takeaway.

The couple secretly tied the knot in London last month – and despite being multimillionaires they decided to keep things very low key.

Speaking about their big day, Georgia revealed she changed into her PJs as soon as it was over and – in scenes that will air on their show – they tucked into a feast.

She explained: “We came back to mine and had a Chinese. It was unreal. It is so me and Tommy. I literally got back, got my PJs on straight away and we had a Chinese. 

“The best bit about it was that we hired tables. So we had a top table…And we all just stuffed our faces with Chinese … it was actually brilliant. So good.”

She continued:  “All of it is filmed, you will get to see everything. When we started the series it wasn’t in the plan. In genuine Georgia and Tommy fashion that is what they ended up getting in the end. It was unbelievable. . .”

At the time, the couple announced the news with a picture of them newlywed alongside son Brody.

They wrote: "1st December… we done a thing.

"After a challenging year. We decided we wanted to end the year happy, in love & together as husband & wife.

"Wedding part 2 coming soon."

They are also still planning to hold a biggest ceremony abroad in 2025. Filming wrapped last month and fans don’t have to wait long to watch the wedding because of the show’s very fast turnaround.

Georgia said: “We finished filming about a month ago. It is filmed as things were happening.

“Otherwise it feels stale…yeah it is out quite quick. It was a really good series. I was dreading it a bit. I just didn’t know if I had the energy for it.

“It is hard. We have always stuck to give the good, bad and ugly. It is amazing to watch but it is hard when you do that every series. It would be nice to sit and have a series where we just talk about stuff. That is not us anyway. But it does take its toll. It ended lovely. So that was good.”
