Sam Thompson and Zara McDermott to 'break America' as a power couple after I'm A Celeb and Strictly

I'M A Celeb champ Sam Thompson and girlfriend Zara McDermott have been tipped to crack America following a bumper year.

Between them Sam, 31, and Zara, 26, appeared on the two biggest shows on TV this year.

While Sam was crowned King of the I'm a Celebrity jungle, Zara took part in Strictly Come Dancing.

Now it's been reported 2024 will be equally as exciting for them.

An insider told new! magazine: "Sam's profile has catapulted since appearing on I'm a Celeb and winning the competition was the cherry on top.

"Plus, Zara's Strictly stint means they are both on their way up. Their real dream though is to crack America."
