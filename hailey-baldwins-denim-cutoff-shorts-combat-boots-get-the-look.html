Hailey Baldwins Denim Cutoff Shorts, Combat Boots: Get the Look

Trend alert: Hailey Baldwin is here with the style of the season. While walking around NYC with rumored beau Justin Bieber, the model has been spotted wearing an off-duty uniform of denim cutoff shorts and combat boots on several occasions, and we have a feeling the leg-baring look is about to be everywhere.

Giving Us a hint of the trend at Coachella in April, Hailey headed to the desert music festival in a pair of frayed shorts and white clodhoppers that she paired with a flirty lace corset. Flash forward to last week, and the model hammed it up for photogs during a Starbucks run on Wednesday, June 13, in which the blonde beauty showed off her gams in cuffed GRLFRND denim shorts and black and white combat boots. Paired with a white t-shirt and oversized jacket, the look was all kinds of laid-back cool. But that’s not to say the denim staple can’t pack a more glam punch as well.
