Finola Hughes is an English actress, TV presenter, businessman, author and dancer best known for her role as Anna Devane in the ABC TV drama All My Children and General Hospital, and her role as Laura in the 1983 Staying Alive, sequel to Saturday night craze. She is also known for her role as the identical twin of Anna Devane, Dr. Alexandra Devane Marick, in All My Children and Patty Halliwell in the supernatural Charmed series. She also played the role of Carol in the final seasons of the NBC sitcom Blossom. Hughes has also appeared in films Aspen Extreme, Above Suspicion and Like Crazy.
