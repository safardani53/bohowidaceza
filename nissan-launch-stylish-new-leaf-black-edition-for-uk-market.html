Nissan launch stylish new LEAF Black edition for UK market

NISSAN has introduced their sleek new LEAF Black Edition to the UK consumer market.

Identified as the world's best-selling electric vehicle, it will retail from £26,890 including the £4,500 government plug-in grant.

Building on the widely popular model, the all-black EV's new exterior features include 16-inch alloy wheels, LED headlamps, black mirror door clamps and rear roof spoiler.

Exclusive exterior styling is designed to compliment the vehicle's intelligent technology, including the NissanConnect EV telematics system and Intelligent Around View Monitor.

The Japanese manufacturer's have modified the chassis specifically for Britain's drivers, and have maintained its instantaneous torque while keeping it a near-silent ride.

“The new LEAF Black Edition perfectly showcases Nissan’s commitment to making smart, premium quality products accessible for all," Gareth Dunsmore, Director of Electric Vehicles at Nissan Europe, said.

"This stylish black number combines dynamic styling, with Nissan’s innovative zero-emission technology to offer customers a city-savvy intelligent mobility solution.

"Its sleek design compliments an exciting drive, delivered through high-class engineering, demonstrating that you don’t have to compromise on style or performance when purchasing an electric car.”

Nissan's limited edition Black model will be on sale only up until August 2017, and the first 200 customers are set to receive a free Wi-Fi hotspot.

The world's most popular EV, there are currently 250,000 Nissan LEAFs on the road.
