A SHOPPER has made a Goodwill score that is being met with jealousy from fellow customers.

The thrifty shopper posted their big find to the Reddit thread ThriftStoreHauls, showing off an 11-piece tea set from Goodwill.

User lowlife9 bought the set for $8, complete with four mugs, a teapot, milk decanter, and sugar pot with two resting plates.

What makes this 11-piece set unique is that it's a Peter Saenger Star Trek The Next Generation Captain Picard set.

Uniqueness, in this case, means money as the set was valued at $400 by Saenger Porcelain.

Star Trek The Next Generation is set 78 years after the original series and ran from 1987 to 1994 over 178 episodes.
