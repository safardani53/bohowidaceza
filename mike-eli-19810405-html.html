Eli Young Band is an American country music group whose members met while they were students at the University of North Texas in Denton, Texas: Mike Eli, James Young, Jon Jones and Chris Thompson. They released their debut album under their name in 2002, followed by the release of Carnival Records in 2005. Their third album, "Jet Black & J Jealous", was released in 2008 on Universal South Records. The second biggest studio album, "Life at Best", was released in 2011 by Republic Nashville, with 10,000 Towns followed in early 2014. The band entered the Billboard country charts eight times, with four singles. Their first place: "Crazy Girl", the best country song of 2011 by Billboard Year-End, with "Even If It Breaks Your Heart", "Drunk Last Night" and "Love Ain't".
