Wednesday A Horror Comedy Series Is Set To Be Released On Netflix Soon

Wednesday, Netflix’s new Addams Family series, is planned to debut on the platform on Wednesday, November 23, 2022, at 3.01 a.m. ET. The program follows the eponymous girl through her school years as she attempts to comprehend her unique psychic abilities and solve different mysteries. Jenna Ortega is the lead in the show, with many others playing crucial supporting parts. It is directed by Tim Burton and co-written by Alfred Gough and Miles Millar.

Wednesday on Netflix promises to be exciting viewing.

The official trailer for Wednesday, October 9, 2022, was released by Netflix, and it provides a preview of the film’s interesting universe filled with mystery. As the narrator states, the video begins with an ominous tone with a vehicle entering the Nevermore Academy.

“Nevermore was founded to provide a secure sanctuary for our children to study and develop, regardless of who or what they are.”

After that, a dramatic background soundtrack begins to play, and the teaser briefly portrays many moments from the series. The video does not disclose much essential information, but it definitely sets the tone and substance of the plot. According to Netflix, below is the official summary of the film:

“Weekend Addams, smart, sarcastic, and a touch dead inside, investigates a murder spree while finding new friends — and opponents — at Nevermore Academy.”

According to the description and trailer, viewers can expect an exciting and engaging series that combines aspects of humor and horror. The Addams Family series has spawned several films and television shows over the years, most notably the 1964 program simply named The Addams Family, which apparently inspired Barry Sonnenfeld’s 1991 film of the same name.

In 1993, a sequel named Addams Family Values was published. Two additional films were released: The Adams Family in 2019, and The Addams Family 2 in 2021. All of the franchise’s films were financially successful and earned largely mixed-to-positive critical reviews.

A short glance at the Wednesday cast

Jenna Ortega plays the lead on Wednesday. Ortega looks fantastic in the title role, effortlessly capturing the idiosyncrasies and intricacies of her character. The actress is expected to provide a strong performance. Aside from Wednesday, Ortega is most recognized for her roles in a variety of notable films and television episodes, including Jane the Virgin, You, and The Fallout, to mention a few. Apart from Ortega, the program also stars Gwendoline Christie as Larissa Weems, Jamie McShane as Sheriff Donovan Galpin, Riki Lindhome as Dr. Valerie Kinbott, and Percy Hynes White as Xavier Thorpe, among many more.

Tim Burton, one of the show’s creators, apparently planned to direct The Addams Family in 1991, however, the production was subsequently headed by another filmmaker. Over the years, Burton has directed numerous legendary pictures, including Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Batman, and Batman Returns, to mention a few.
