Alan Bersten Celeb Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Alan Bersten (born May 26, 1994, in Minnetonka, Minnesota) is an American dancer who competed on Season 24 of Dancing with the Stars with actress Heather Morris while her partner Maksim Chmerkovskiy recovered from an injury. He got his start competing on Season 10 of So You Think You Can Dance with Amy Yakima. He teaches at Dance With Us America and attended the University of Minnesota.

Best Known For:
Alan is best known for competing on Season 24 of Dancing with the Stars and Season 10 of So You Think You Can Dance.

Personal Life:
Alan was born in Minnetonka, Minnesota, though his family is from Russia. The dancer is the youngest of three siblings. His older brother Gene also auditioned to be a contestant on Season 10 of So You Think You Can Dance. Alan was taught to dance by his older brother when he was seven.
