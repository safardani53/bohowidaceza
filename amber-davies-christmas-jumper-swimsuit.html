Amber Davies shows off her stunning figure in Christmas jumper-themed swimsuit

LOVE Island’s Amber Davies has a yarning for warmer weather this festive season — and has the Christmas jumper-themed swimsuit to prove it.

And who can blame the 27-year-old West End performer as she prepares to feel the chill as a contestant on ITV’s Dancing On Ice next month.

The limited edition swimwear range is launched today by easyJet Holidays, with the £19.99 cost redeemable against its package holidays.

Amber has been paired with new Dancing on Ice pro, Simon Proulx-Senecal, and it seems they are a match made in heaven.

She called him: "The best partner one could wish for."

Amber, who won the ITV2 reality series with ex Kem Cetinay, is hoping to follow in the footsteps of her fellow Islanders who have reached the final of the popular dance contest.

Her ex Kem and Wes Nelson all made it fan in the contest with Liberty Poole, Maura Higgins and Ekin-Su also having donned their skates in a bid to lift the trophy.

Theatre star Amber is currently loved-up with fellow West End performer Ben Joyce after meeting whilst performing together in Back to the Future.

She previously dated Nick Kyriaco on and off for almost three years before they split in October 2022.
