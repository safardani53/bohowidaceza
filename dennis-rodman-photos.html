Dennis Rodman: Photos Hollywood Life

Dennis Rodman, born May 13, 1961 in Trenton, New Jersey, is an athlete who’s wild style and busy personal life almost eclipsed his talent on the court and you can see photos of him here.

He was known for his fierce defensive style and over his NBA career he played for the Detroit Pistons, San Antonio Spurs, Chicago Bulls, Los Angeles Lakers, and Dallas Mavericks. Dennis also had a reputation for erratic behavior and sometimes clashed with his teams. His last season in the NBA was in 2000, but played abroad and in minor leagues sporadically in years after.

In addition to his basketball career, Dennis dabbled in pro wrestling and acting. He was in the nWo wrestling league and his film credits include 1997’s ‘Double Team’, 1999’s ‘Simon Sez’ and a cameo in 2007’s ‘The Comebacks.’ Dennis was the subject of the 2019 documentary ‘Rodman: For Better or Worse’.

Dennis dated some of the hottest women of the 90s. He reportedly dated Madonna, and enjoyed a quickie marriage to bombshell Carmen Electra. Dennis has three children.
