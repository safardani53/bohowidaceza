Orlando Bloom opens up on those naked paddle board pictures with Katy Perry and denies they were a p

HE is as famous now for his naked paddle boarding with ex-girlfriend KATY PERRY as for his Hollywood movies.

And ORLANDO BLOOM has broken his silence on that full-frontal Italian sail in August last year, denying the snaps were a publicity stunt.

Speaking to Elle magazine, out tomorrow, the actor said: “It was extremely surprising.

“I wouldn’t have put myself in that position if I’d thought it would happen.

“I’ve been photographed a million times in a million different ways. I have a good radar. We’d been completely alone for five days with nothing around us.

“So I had a moment of feeling free . . . What can I tell you?”

Their high-profile romance hit the rocks after a year together, with the pair confirming in February that they had gone their separate ways.

Harry losing Number 1 'Ed start

HARRY STYLES’ first solo No1 isn’t in the bag yet – far from it.

On Monday Harry was out-selling ED SHEERAN’s Shape Of You by double, shifting nearly 30,000 copies of debut single Sign Of The Times compared to Ed’s 13,988.

But now the latest streaming figures have been included, second-placed Ed has closed the gap to fewer than 10,000.

With Ed continuing to rack up far more listens than Harry on Spotify, he could easily overtake his chum at the top of the charts.

A source revealed: “Sign Of The Times is only at No12 on Spotify overall. That might not be enough when the streaming ratio is properly added come the end of the week.

“Shape Of You is still getting 488,000 daily streams to Harry’s 277,000.

“It is now an incredibly tight race. It’ll be fascinating to see who comes out on top on Friday.”

There’s one thing for sure – Harry won’t want to settle for runner-up.
