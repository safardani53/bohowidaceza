Lidl is about to sell Xbox One S and THREE games for just 129 in unbelievable Black Friday deal

GAMERS are being treated to a cracking Xbox deal this Black Friday.

Supermarket chain Lidl are offering you an Xbox One S with 3 games for just £129 when the dazzling day of discounts rolls around on November 29.

That's a huge 32 per cent discount on the typical £189.99 price tag.

There is one catch though – the console is the Digital version of the Xbox One S.

That means it has no disk drive, so you'll have to download all your games.

First reported by HotUKDeals, the price cut was later confirmed by Lidl in an email to The Sun.

A spokesperson said it will be available on Black Friday only (November 29) and only while stocks last.

The console comes with a gargantuan 1 terabyte of storage as well as a wireless controller.

Not only that, but you'll get Minecraft, Sea of Thieves and Forza Horizon 3 thrown in to boot.

They'll come as digital download cards rather than discs, for obvious reasons.
