Howie Mandel looks completely unrecognizable with full beard in new video as fans praise 'legendary'

HOWIE Mandel has shocked fans after he appeared with a full beard in a new video.

His followers praised the legendary look.

On Friday, Howie shared a video to his Instagram.

It featured him walking out from behind stage, with the camera faced toward him.

The 67-year-old had on a backward baseball cap, glasses, and a T-shirt.

While the America's Got Talent judge has been growing a beard lately, it has mostly been contained to his upper lip and chin.

His beard in this video, however, was a full beard that reached from cheek to cheek and was longer in length.

The comedian looked to be on the set of AGT as he talked with a crew member.

However, what he was saying could not be heard by his followers because he played the song Beard Gang by ReddTheRapper over it.

Howie simply captioned the video: "#beard."

This is not the first time this year that Howie dramatically changed up his look.

In July, he revealed a new hairdo, just as he announced a break from social media.

Howie took the video from inside his massive home closet.

He was wearing a black T-shirt with a small graphic design on the chest and his classic round glasses.

In addition to the causal outfit, the former game show host also had a long and floppy brown hair piece on top of his typically bald head.

The hair on Howie appeared to be about five inches long, and it was flopping all over the place.

In the clip, he said, "Just a note, I'm going to take a break from posting just for this one day. Okay?"
