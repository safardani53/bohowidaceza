TRISTAN Tate is known for being the brother of Andrew Tate.

Does Tristan Tate have children?

Tristan is a former kickboxer, businessman and social media influencer.

Born on July 15, 1988, Tristan Tate is the younger brother of Andrew Tate, who also grew up in their home town of Luton.

He first rose to fame in 2011, after starring in reality TV show Shipwrecked.

After that, he retired from kickboxing and moved to Romania with his brother to focus on their business empire.

Tristan Tate has mentioned that he has children in interviews, but has said he doesn't talk about how many he has and what their names are.

He has however shared details of his pet dogs, posting in July 2019 that he'd rescued homeless dogs, Loka and Baily, from the street.

Who are Tristan Tate's parents?

Tristan has two siblings, his older brother Andrew and a sister, Janine.
