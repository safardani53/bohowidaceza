Vanna White, 66, shows off her figure in skintight gown in new Celebrity Wheel of Fortune sneak peek

VANNA White has shown off her slim figure in a tight blue gown in a Celebrity Wheel of Fortune season preview.

She appeared in a quick video posted to Instagram standing next to host Pat Sajak to tease who would be showing up in the show's premiere.

Vanna stood sparkling in a glittering blue sleeveless dress.

Pat was right beside her as the pair bantered about the next episode of Celebrity Wheel of Fortune.

The skintight dressed hugged Vanna's curves as she stood with her hands clasped.

She wore her blond hair with darker lowlights down in loose waves around her face.

Meanwhile, Pat made a bit of a shady joke at Vanna's expense.

One contestant named Beryl missed out on a big $100,000 but got a consolation prize from Vanna instead.

Pat revealed to the audience that Beryl had met Vanna prior to her appearance in the competition.

Beryl confirmed that she met Vanna at the Myrtle Beach Airport in South Carolina in 2015.

"[When I met her] she was so kind [and] she let me take a picture with her," Beryl confessed.

She reiterated that though Vanna was really nice, Vanna refused to share the pound cake that the TV personality carried with her with Beryl.

After Beryl recounted her and Vanna's interaction Beryl sadly lost out on the grand prize.

"Well, you didn't get the $100,000, but instead, you're replacing Vanna," Pat joked with Beryl in the credits scene. "No not at all!" he then assured.

He also playfully insisted that Vanna needed to "make it up" to Beryl for not sharing her pound cake at the airport.

Being the good sport she is, Vanna walked on stage and presented Beryl with a slice of pound cake.

According to TMZ, White got a substantial pay increase with the deal, though the exact figure is undisclosed.

Vanna - who makes $3million a season - had reportedly been seeking a raise from the show’s producers, claiming that she only received bonuses for the last 18 years.
