SUMMER’S here so it’s time to purchase that perfect hot weather wardrobe.

And now with so many shops offering a more inclusive sizing range there’s plenty of options for everyone, no matter your body shape or size.

As proven by one TikTok video, when Poppy who is a size 22, and her friend, Nina, a size 10, tried on a number of the same dress from New Look - with both of them looking amazing.

The first dress shown by the duo is the golden hour pink floral metallic chiffon wrap dress, which is available from size 8 through to size 28.

Both pair this dress with the bright blue quilted leather-look chain shoulder bag.

Their next look is the beach to aperitif blue paisley puff sleeve maxi dress, which the pair both wear in an off-the-shoulder style, with the off white quilted embellished tote bag.

Next the fashionistas show off the jetsetter pink zig zag fine knit kimono.

The finally item the pair try on is the sunrise to sunset pink lemon chiffon frill midi dress, which they each pair with a different coloured plaited strap shoulder bag.

The duo end the video telling their viewers to “wear what makes you happy!”

Fashion fans were loving the styles the two friends showed off, and the fact the summer New Look range is so size inclusive.
