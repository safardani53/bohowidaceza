Chloe Ferry's mum looks exactly like her after surgery as she poses for glam snap

CHLOE Ferry's lookalike mum is the spitting image of the Geordie Shore star after undergoing surgery.

Liz, 60, is unrecognisable from a few years ago before she underwent a drastic makeover.

Now she looks exactly like her reality star daughter as she posed for a glam snap.

Like Chloe, Liz has had work done including her lip and face fillers and bright white veneers.

When she first appeared on the show in series 12 in 2016, Liz had a small gap between her front teeth.

She also sported brunette hair usually scraped back, glasses on a string around her neck and was partial to wearing practical jumpers.

But for her 59th birthday Chloe treated her to a new set of veeners.

She now has glamorous bleached blonde hair, a plump pout and a whole new chic wardrobe.

She also looks to have lost weight and has mastered makeup contouring, HD eyebrows and wearing fake eyelashes.

The pair have become near-identical since Chloe moved out of her £1.1 million mansion and back with her mum.
