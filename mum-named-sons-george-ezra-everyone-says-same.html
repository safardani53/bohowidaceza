I named my sons before a singer got famous, and now everyone always says the same thing

SHE'D been hoping that the names she'd settled on for her sons were pretty unique.

But after one singer shot to success, Violet-May now finds that people say the same thing to her all the time about her sons' monikers.

Violet-May took to TikTok to share a video in which she revealed the comment she always hears as she mimicked a conversation with herself starting with: "Oh two lovely boys - what are their names?"

"George and Ezra," she replied.

"OMG like the singer?" comes the usual response.

However, Violet-May insisted in her caption that the similarity to singer George Ezra's name was entirely accidental.

"This all I get! Wasn't intentional though," she wrote.

People were quick to comment on the video to admit their own near misses with names.

"My little boy is called George and his middle name was going to be Micheal until I realised," one wrote.

"My sister's sons are called keanu and reece, she didn’t even notice anything until they were 2 and someone pointed out they sound like keanu reeves," another added.

While a third commented: "My parents get it a lot. They called my sister Grace, our surname is Kelly."

"I have a Hudson, and loved the name River for my baby girl but I just couldn’t bring myself to be calling ‘Hudson, River," someone else wrote.

"Literally pregnant and I have a George and if it’s a boy I really want the name Ezra!! I’m so worried everyone’s going take the mick out of me!!" another mum-to-be added.

To which Violet-May replied: "Omg no way - do it!!!"

And in response to another comment, she insisted that despite all the remarks - she still loves her boys' names.
