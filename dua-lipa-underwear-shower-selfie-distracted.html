Dua Lipa flashes her underwear in sexy shower selfie - but fans are all distracted by steamy detail

DUA Lipa looked stunning as she stripped to a lace thong for a sexy shower selfie - but fans were left more distracted by a very steamy detail in the background.

The One Kiss singer, 27, slipped into black high-waisted underwear and an oversized khaki t-shirt for a sultry snap.

Dua showed off her toned figure and natural look in a mirror selfie - ditching her make-up and sweeping her dark tresses back.

But some eagle-eyed fans were quick to spot telling hand prints on the shower glass, leaving fans to draw their own conclusions.

One fan wrote: "Those shower marks," with a cheeky devil emoji.

Another posted: "Oh looks like things got very steamy in the shower," with a wink emoji.

A third said: "Look at the finger marks on the shower," while another quipped: "There was nothing clean going on in that shower from what I see."

Someone else cheekily asked: "Wonder what she was doing inside that shower?"

Earlier this week, The Sun revealed that Dua had signed her most expensive brand deal to date and will soon be unveiled as the new face of Porsche.

Insiders say the Levitating singer, who was the face of luxury car company Jaguar, has been offered a seven-figure sum to sign on the dotted line.

A source said: “Dua is one of the hottest faces in music right now and is a global star.

“She has a huge brand pulling power and Porsche offered her a seven-figure package she could not turn down.

“Dua first started working with Jaguar in 2018 and it’s made her a huge amount of money.

“But when Porsche came knocking, it was a really exciting offer and she is looking forward to getting involved."

Dua’s first love is music and with 12 UK top ten singles, including three No1s, under her belt, she will be hoping to add to those figures.

Her first, self-titled, album in 2017 and 2020’s Future Nostalgia both went straight to the top spot and Dua will be hoping her third record follows suit.

Earlier this year The Sun revealed that Dua had poured her heart and soul into the album and had written every song on the track list, with a possible major tour to support it in 2024.
