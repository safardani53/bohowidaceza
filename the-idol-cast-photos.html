Photos Of The Weeknd &amp; Lily-Rose Depps New Show Hollywood Life

‘The Idol’ is a brand-new series from ‘Euphoria’ creator Sam Levinson, The Weeknd, and Reza Fahim. The show stars Lily-Rose Depp and The Weeknd as Jocelyn and Tedros.

The official synopsis for the drama series reads: “After a nervous breakdown derailed Jocelyn’s last tour, she’s determined to claim her rightful status as the greatest and sexiest pop star in America. Her passions are reignited by Tedros, a nightclub impresario with a sordid past. Will her romantic awakening take her to glorious new heights or the deepest and darkest depths of her soul?”

‘The Idol’ is set to premiere at the Cannes Film Festival before it debuts June 4 on HBO. The series also stars Troye Sivan, Dan Levy, Da’Vine Joy Randolph, Eli Roth, Hari Nef, Jane Adams, Jennie Ruby Jane, Mike Dean, Moses Sumney, Rachel Sennott, Ramsey, Suzanna Son, and Hank Azaria.
