Matthew Koma Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Matthew Koma (born Matthew Bair in Seaford, Long Island, New York on June 2, 1987) is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, and DJ. He was inspired by the music of Elvis Costello and Bruce Springsteen and started writing music at the age of nine. Matt first started his music career in punk rock, but later transitioned into a large range of genres, from pop and hip hop to EDM. His early collaborations include work with Hardwell, Zedd, Miriam Bryant, Alesso, Tiësto, LMFAO, and Ryan Tedder of One Republic. After Matthew was noticed by an acoustic performance of “She” by Elvis Costello, he was then signed to Interscope Records. He released his first EP, Parachute in 2012, in which he wrote and produced all four tracks. Since then, the singer has toured with LMFAO with Far East Movement, Owl City, and Ellie Goulding with Charlie XCX. Matthew has also collaborated with many other artists, including Tiesto on their album a Town Called Paradise. The talented musician is currently working on his album Arcadia.

Best Known For:
Matthew is best known as a sought-after producer in music.

Personal Life:
Matthew started dating actress Hilary Duff in early 2017.
