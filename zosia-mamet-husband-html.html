5 Years and Counting A Look into Zosia Mamet and Husband Evan Jonigkeits Happy Marriage

Zosia Mamet has been managing a happy marriage with her husband, Evan Jonigkeit, for more than five years.

The actress, who played the role of Annie Mouradian in the television series The Flight Attendant, and her husband, also an actor, tied the knot back in October 2016.

Since then, the couple has hardly missed an opportunity to show off their adorable chemistry.

Whether it is walking down the red carpet or posting pictures on social media, the two ensure to make people gaze at them in awe.

Zosia Mamet and Husband Evan Jonigkeit Are Show-Stoppers

Mamet and Jonigkeit attended the star-studded premiere for The Night House in New York City in August 2021.

The Girls star flaunted her floor-length teal silk gown with a ruched mock neckline as she posed on the red carpet with her husband.

In addition, she rocked crisscross orange sandals and matched them with some teal shadow on her lids and a muted orange lipstick on her pout.

Her husband dashed a grey-blue suit jacket layered over a white dress shirt and a pair of matching trousers.

As they graced the red carpet, the couple could hardly keep their hands off one another. They were always close to each other and held each other’s hands.

Likewise, Jonigkeit’s Instagram post in October 2020 also made the rounds because of its heart-touching caption.

Zosia Mamet and her husband Evan Jonigkeit were photographed at their wedding. (Photo: Instagram)

“Happy anniversary @zosiamamet you’re to the best thing that’s happened to me. As we continue to live a charmed life and sometimes weather storms together, you are my safe shelter,” the Sweetbitter star wrote, expressing his love for his beloved wife.

The post, marking the couple’s fourth wedding anniversary, featured a picture of them apparently taken on the wedding day.

Zosia Mamet and Evan Jonigkeit’s Rustic Wedding

While Mamet and Jonigkeit’s marriage has been a wonderful story, their wedding was also no less than a blockbuster movie.

They reportedly had an intimate wedding ceremony at Cedar Lakes Estates in Port Jervis, New York.

In a Vogue issue, the actress revealed the details, saying, “The guests all had a good time at the relatively small reception of around 100 people, most of which was family.”

Some guests included the couple’s close friends, Alaina Moore, Patrick Riley, Tommy Agriodimas, and Christina Lonsdale.

Interestingly, one of their guests even made temporary tattoos for everyone at the wedding. The tattoo was a heart with “E+Z” written inside it.

“We all talked, sang, danced, and midnight-snacked until the wee hours of the morning. It was perfect,” Mamet added.

In addition, she revealed she must have tried on 40 dresses before selecting a black Givenchy gown for the big day.

“I wanted something comfortable, and I didn’t really want to wear white,” she explained.

The Dickinson actress and her spouse also danced to Neil Young’s ‘Harvest Moon’ to kick start the party after the wedding rituals.
