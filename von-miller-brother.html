Parents 

Miller brothers were born to their parents, Gloria Miller and Von B’VSean Miller Sr. Fun fact, both parents were athletes but never took sports as their mainstream career. 

Their mother, Gloria Miller (born on July 13, 1965), used to be a high school volleyball player and was indulged in the high school’s track and field team. She has been the major support pillar in the boys’ lives. 

Similarly, their father, Von B’VSean Miller Sr., used to be a high school football player. He was once a manager at Payless Shoes Store. He enrolled at a technical school and worked for two battery firms.

While their father was busy with work, their mother would drive them to every single one of Von’s games, either at high school, college, or with the Broncos. When Von stated that he wanted to play football, she supported him.

A Brief Introduction to Von Miller 

Fully named Vonnie B’VSean Miller and elder brother of Vince Miller, Von is a popular name in the NFL industry. He currently plays for the Buffalo Bills as the outside linebacker. 

He has collected many awards and accolades under his name. Talking about his academic background, he attended DeSoto High School and later graduated from Texas A&M University. 


Besides his playing career, Von Miller is highly invested in charitable works. He runs Von’s Vision Foundation, which provides low-income students with eye care and fashionable corrective eyewear.

His foundation is currently in collaboration with the international eyewear retailer Etnia Barcelona.  

Unlike his brother Vince, Von is quite active on social media sites. To know more about him, check him out on Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube.
