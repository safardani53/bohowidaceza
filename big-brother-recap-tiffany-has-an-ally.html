Big Brother Recap: Tiffany Has An Ally!

Being Vanessa from Big Brother Season 17’s sister is ruining Tiffany’s game. 

No one really trusts her and the only reason she was kept around was to annoy Frank, but was it really worth it?

Tiffany clicked on pretty quickly why she was kept around and refused to thank anyone for keeping her around. 

She was rude to Frank, who informed her that Da’vonne was the one who told her about the Fatal Five alliance. Da’vonne argued with him in public that she was not the one who told him. 

Tiffany knew Da’vonne was lying because Da’vonne was the first person she asked about creating the alliance. This is when Tiffany realized she had no one in the house and set off to the have not room to have a cry. 

Frank went in and chatted with her and they made the most unlikely alliance in Big Brother history, but Da’vonne walked in and crashed the party. 

Nicole went to the room after Frank left, but Tiffany made it very clear she knew the reason Nicole was there, so Nicole got up and stormed back out of the room. 

Tiffany then went and met Da’vonne in another room and things got confrontational. Zakiyah stormed in to find out what the heck was going on, but Tiffany just wanted to make it clear that she isn’t an idiot. 

After Zakiyah stormed off, Da’vonne forced Tiffany to take a break from the game and let all of her emotions out because it’s going to ruin her game. 

Tiffany agreed and the two of them made a deal. 

Paulie made it clear to his alliance members that they made a mistake keeping Tiffany and that he wouldn’t make the same mistake again this week. They all agreed with him. 

Paulie nominated Tiffany and Natalie for eviction.

Tiffany and Frank were conspiring straight away and Tiffany realized she needed to shake the foundations of Paulie’s alliance, so she made it her mission to win the Road Kill. 

The unthinkable happened and she actually managed to eke out a victory over her fellow houseguests. 

She informed Da’vonne that she was putting Corey up and to keep her mouth shut about it. Da’vonne kept it zipped in case she was put up instead. 

At the Road Kill ceremony Corey laughed when he was put up. It was like he figured it all out in that instant. 

Is there a chance that Tiffany could really survive another week?
