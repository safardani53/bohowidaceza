30 Aldi finds starting at $4 - including adorable outdoor essential with lots of different option

WITH bargains starting at $4.49, one Aldi fan has shared her best home and clothing finds at the discount store.

Katie Kelly, who goes by @bitsbitesblog on TikTok, recently took her followers along for a trip to her local store in Chicago, Illinois.

Her website - bitsandbitesblog.com - is dedicated to deals, especially at Aldi.

In this video, she picked out 30 of the best Aldi finds across home and garden, apparel, kitchenware, and frozen food.

Below are nine of her favorites.

Please note prices and products may vary from store to store.

“Their spring gnomes are back,” noticed Katie. “Of course! People flock to these.”

There are also 16-inch gnomes that make ideal spring decor for the mantel.

A variety of designs are available and each is $4.99

The electric griddle from Ambiano is a steal at $24.99

Aldi is down with farmhouse style this week and the pitchers are a standout choice.

4. OVERSIZED SERVING BOWL

This massive wooden serving bowl from Crofton is just $19.99.

“I really like this,” Katie said as she picked it up to inspect the quality.

5. LOUNGEWEAR ROMPER

Katie described this find from the Serra brand as “super comfy.”

6. KIDS’ BOOTS

There are also deals to be found on children’s apparel this week, such as Lil & Dan rain boots that come in “lots of different options.”

The waterproof rubber boots are just $12.99 a pair with designs such as trucks, dino, alligator, ice cream, floral, and unicorn.

7. PET RAIN GEAR

Aldi is even helping furry friends stay dry amidst spring showers.

The Heart to Tail brand Pet Rain Boots and Pet Rain Jacket are both $6.99 each and come in a selection of print patterns.

8. CHEESE BOARD

Katie particularly liked the marble inlay on the cheese serving board from Crofton.

At $16.99 it would be a perfect item for laying out a charcuterie board at home.

9. PIZZA

This week, Katie also picked up a quick dinner.

The low-carb and gluten-free 12-inch veggie supreme cauliflower crust was $1 off.

There are other cauliflower crust flatbreads and pizzas available depending on the stock at your local store.
