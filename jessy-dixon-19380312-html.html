Jessy Dixon (Gospel Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Jessy Dixon is an American singer, songwriter and pianist, with many hits with racial audiences. He won seven Grammy Award nominations during his career. Musicians he has worked with include Paul Simon, Andrae Crouch, DeGarmo & Key and most recently Bill Gaither in the Homecoming concert series. He wrote songs for Amy Grant, Natalie Cole, Cher and Diana Ross. Dixon is an appointed minister at Calvary Ministries International in Fort Wayne, Indiana.
