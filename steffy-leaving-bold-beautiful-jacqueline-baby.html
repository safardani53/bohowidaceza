Is Steffy leaving The Bold and the Beautiful?

THE BOLD and the Beautiful is a popular soap opera that first premiered in 1987.

During seasons 35 and 36, fans began to worry about the future of Steffy Forrester, played by Jacqueline MacInnes Wood, and now they want to know if she's leaving.

Is Steffy leaving The Bold and the Beautiful?

Fans speculated Steffy was leaving the show during season 35 when fans saw her in bed with Finn reminiscing on their love story which fans have viewed as a sign that Steffy might be killed off.

Steffy later confronted Sheila and told her to stay away from Taylor and Thomas, adding that she would never be a part of the Forrester family.

While she was absent for a long duration of season 35 and most of season 36, Steffy doesn't seem to be leaving the show.

Is Steffy pregnant in real life again?

On April 17, 2023, Jacqueline MacInnes Wood announced she was pregnant with her fourth child.

"I feel like every time I'm here I'm always announcing that I'm pregnant!" she joked while appearing on The Talk on Monday.

Wood's pregnancy news comes less than one year after the birth of her third child, Brando, in May 2021. 

"...Elan and I dreamed of having a large family as we are both only children and are over the moon that our dreams are coming true," she told People at the time.

Jacqueline gave birth to her son on August 27, 2023.

She announced her family's newest edition with a sweet photo on Instagram.

"Thank you for all the messages. Just been taking this time to slow down and soak in this moment. #gratefulheart."

As of September 2023, the actress is away from the show on maternity leave.

How many children does Jacqueline MacInnes Wood have?

The Bold and the Beautiful actress has been married to talent agent, Elan Ruspoli, since 2018.

Throughout the course of their marriage, they have welcomed three boys together - Rise, Lenix, and Brando.

When talking about her firstborn, Wood revealed that his name is a symbol of growth.
