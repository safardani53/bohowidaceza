A BEAUTY fan who loves to save money has shared how she snagged name-brand products for a fraction of the price.

While at her local Dollar Tree, she showed viewers what to look out for at the discount store so that they could grab popular makeup items.

Kim Nuzzolo (@kimnuzzolo) shares affordable beauty finds at discounted prices online.

She ducked into a dollar store to show viewers her tips to hit the "makeup jackpot" in a video.

She panned across the aisles as she showed the vast selection of products in the superstore.

"Back with another Dollar Tree, how to find name-brand makeup in the makeup aisle," she said.

According to the budget-friendly shopper, there are specific things to pay attention to.

She first told her audience to "look out for the black and white packages."

Kim held up a Revlon lipstick in a gorgeous red color that she added would be great for the holidays.

She also spotted Flower Beauty lip glosses. without any packaging, as well as NYX liquid lipsticks and Rimmel lip products that were also in black and white packaging.
