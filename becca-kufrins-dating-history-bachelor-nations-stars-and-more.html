Becca Kufrins Dating History: Bachelor Nations Stars and More

Becca Kufrin found The One on reality TV — but it took a few attempts.

Kufrin was engaged to Arie Luyendyk Jr. and Garrett Yrigoyen before giving Bachelor Nation romances one more shot on Bachelor in Paradise in summer 2021. At the time, she joked that “third times the charm” after meeting Thomas Jacobs on the sands of Mexico.

Despite briefly breaking up during the show’s October 2021 finale, the twosome reconnected off camera and have been going strong ever since.

“I’m sorry for breaking up with you on national television, but I’ll take every day to make it up to you, Tommy,” Kufrin captioned an Instagram video after the finale aired in October 2021. “Thanks for making my heart smile far more than it ever has. Now time to start this real life.”
