What was Matthew Perry's net worth in 2023?

Matthew Perry not only starred in the iconic Friends series, but he also made appearances in film and TV favourites throughout his career.

He starred in movies like The Ron Clark Story, Fools Rush In, The Whole Nine Yards, and 17 Again.

How much was Matthew Perry paid for Friends?

The TV icon played the well-recognized character, Chandler Bing, in the 90s Friends sitcom, which aired for over ten years.

In 2002, Perry and his five co-stars made history by striking the first-ever $1million per-episode pay deal, according to US Weekly.

The cast negotiated together and threatened to quit the show if they were not paid the same.

At the time, it was the largest-ever paycheck for a 30-minute-long TV show.

In the early days of Friends, according to MarketPlace, Perry, Jennifer Aniston, Lisa Kudrow, Courtney Cox, Matt LeBlanc, and David Schwimmer were reportedly paid $22,500 per episode.

But by the third season, the group was reportedly making $100,000 per episode.

Perry is reported to have earned at least $90million from his decade on Friends.

But his payout from Friends didn't stop when the series ended.

In 2004 when the show came to an end, the clever negotiation skills of the cast meant they have each made significant profits in the years since thanks to syndication payments.

While Warner Bros earns about $1billion a year from reruns of the show, USA Today reported in 2015, the six cast members were pocketing around 2 per cent of that - an annual income of about $20million.
