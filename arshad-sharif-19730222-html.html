Arshad Sharif (Journalist) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Arshad Sharif Urdu: ارشد شریف, Pakistani journalist, writer and television reporter. He specializes in drafting investigative papers and reporting on numerous national political events for national and international news organizations, primarily for the UK. On March 23, 2019, he received the Pride Award for his achievements from the President of Pakistan for his contributions to the field of journalism. Arshad Sharif currently hosts and primarily hosts PowerPlay on ARY News and is known as the “Dog Malik Riaz”. Sharif served on AAJ News as news director. Prior to joining Aaj, he headed the Dunya TV press group as news director and Kyun host.
