Andre Roberson Girlfriend Rachel DeMita: Dating Timeline

Rachel DeMita, the girlfriend of the former NBA player Andre Roberson, has been a steadfast support in his career as he navigated the complexities of a professional basketball career.

Time and again, the basketball couple has captivated the attention of fans and followers alike with their relationship.

Let’s delve into the dating timeline of Andre Roberson and Rachel Demita, unraveling the story behind their connection.

Andre Roberson is an esteemed American professional basketball player known for his defensive performances.

Born on December 4, 1991, Roberson embarked on his basketball journey at Karen Wagner High School in San Antonio.

The three-star recruit joined the University of Colorado Boulder and was named first-team All-Pac-12 as a junior in 2012–13.

A Colorado Buffaloes standout, Roberson was selected by the Minnesota Timberwolves in the 2013 NBA draft.  

He was later acquired by the Oklahoma City Thunder, where he rose to prominence as a defensive specialist.

His career hit a major setback when he suffered a left patellar tendon injury in 2018. He persevered through a grueling recovery process, making a brief comeback in 2020.

Similarly, Roberson last played for Oklahoma City Blue in 2023 following a brief stint with the Brooklyn Nets in 2021.

Meet Andre Roberson Girlfriend: Rachel DeMita

Rachel Annamarie DeMita, a prominent TV show host, producer, and digital creator, has made waves in the world of sports and lifestyle content.

She was born on June 14, 1990, and grew up as the only child of Jack and Shannon DeMita.

Her passion for basketball emerged during her school days at Wadsworth High School, where she actively played the game.

After graduating, Rachel earned a full scholarship to Dominion University but later transferred to American University.

As per reports, she pursued a degree in broadcast journalism and completed her Bachelor of Arts in 2011. 

Likewise, Rachel gained fame after she became the host of the first-ever broadcast of the NBA 2K18 video game.

Throughout her career, Rachel has made significant contributions to various media platforms. The list includes NBA TV, Yahoo, Fox Sports Digital, ESPN, Uproxx, and Bleacher Report.

Notably, she served as the host of Dunk League Season 3 and has collaborated with renowned brands such as ADIDAS, Eastbay, and Finish Line.

Rachel’s YouTube career has also flourished despite her rising involvement in the sports industry.

Her channel has amassed over 280,000 subscribers, while her Instagram boasts a following of over a million.

Additionally, she hosts the “Courtside Club” podcast, engaging with prominent athletes, celebrities, and creative minds.

How Long Have They Been Dating?

Andre Roberson has been romantically involved with the accomplished TV personality and digital creator Rachel DeMita since 2018.

Despite initially keeping their relationship under wraps, the couple eventually made it public with an Instagram post in June 2018.

While the exact start date of their relationship remains undisclosed, their affectionate bond quickly became evident to the public.

Rachel and Andre radiate chemistry, often seen enjoying each other’s company both on and off the basketball court.

They frequently share glimpses of their relationship with their followers on social media platforms. It primarily includes outings and basketball-related activities.

In a recent video, Andre shared his experience playing for the Philippines’ Strong Group Team in the 33rd Dubai International Basketball Championship.

Although their team lost in the final, Rachel’s support was palpable, and even appreciated the team for an amazing run.

As of now, Rachel and Andre have not announced any plans for marriage or engagement, nor do they share any children.

However, their devoted relationship and shared experiences hint at the possibility of future milestones together.
